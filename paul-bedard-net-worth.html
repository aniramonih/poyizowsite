Paul Bedard Wiki Biography

Paul Bedard was born in 1968 in the United States of America, and is a reality television personality, lifeguard and alligator catcher, best known for being part of the Animal Planet reality show “Gator Boys”, and in particular for using ways to capture alligators without harming them, and releasing them back into the wild. All of his efforts have helped put his net worth to where it is today.

How rich is Paul Bedard? As of mid-2017, sources estimate a net worth that is at $500,000, mostly earned through a successful career on television. “Gator Boys” has become very popular, and through also appearin in other television shows. As he continues his career it is expected that his wealth will also continue to increase.

Paul Bedard Net Worth $500,000

Growing up, Paul had a passion for animals. He started exploring nature and wildlife at a young age, and caught his first snake when he was 12 years old. He attended and matriculated from Bishop Stang Catholic High School in Massachusetts.

He first found popularity when he was cast as part of the reality show “Gator Boys” on Animal Planet, which started in 2012 and aired six episodes. The series follows him alongside Jimmy Riffle – they get calls from the Florida Everglades area which is well known to have many alligators in the wild, but they respond to calls from people who have alligators wandering in their property. The two then work to capture these animals without harming them, and release them back in to the wild, including tackling alligators in backyards and swimming pools. The show has become very popular and has helped increase his net worth significantly. The show has been renewed for a total of six seasons with their last episode filmed in 2014, but while no new season has been filmed, episodes of the show continue to air.

Aside from “Gator Boys”, Bedard also makes frequent guest appearances in talk shows. He’s also made an appearance in the television show “Tanked” as part of an episode, which is another reality series airing on Animal Planet which follows the operations of one of the world’s largest manufacturers of customized aquariums. In 2012, he had a small role in the Hong Kong film “Naked Soldier” which he played the head of a mercenary group. The film is the third installment of the “Naked” series and follows “Naked Weapon”. When he’s not catching alligators, he works as a lifeguard. He is also known to work at Everglades Holiday Park where he educates people about alligators. All of these other opportunities have helped in building his net worth.

For his personal life, there is no information on any relationships. It is known that Paul enjoys Ironman races, preferring the long run, bike, and swim distance of these races. He is a Ford Ironman World Championship winner. In 2004, he had an accident which got him a lacerated kidney and infection, due to which his heart now pumps less oxygen, and his mobility has become limited. Despite that, the injuries have not prevented his desire for racing. He’s also very active on social media, particularly Facebook.
