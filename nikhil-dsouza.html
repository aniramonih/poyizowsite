Some Lesser Known Facts About Nikhil D’Souza

  • He made his debut as a playback singer with his hit single “sham” in Rajshree Ojha’s movie Aisha(2010).
  • Nikhil D’Souza’s major influences were Nick Drake, Jeff Buckley and Eric Clapton.
  • He is currently signed with a London based record label – East west Records which a subsidiary to Warner Music UK and has already released three songs with them which are Silver, Gold and Beautiful mind.
  • He got featured in Rolling Stone Music Magazine(India) several times due to his catchy melancholic sounds.
  • He also got featured on MTV’s Coke Studio Season 3 (2013) where he sang Moh along with Hitesh Sonik, Pandit Sanjeev Abhyankar.
  • He performed two of his original songs – shades of grey and still in love at Sofar Sounds Mumbai.
  • In an interview he said is a self -trained singer and guitarist.
