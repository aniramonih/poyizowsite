Young Devyn Biography, Age, Songs, Net Worth, Height, Boyfriend

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 5, 2024

Young Devyn is a 21-year-old rapper, singer, and songwriter from Brooklyn, New York. She is popularly known for her songs “Nobody,” “To You,” and “Elite.”

Profile Summary

Early Life & Background

Devyn Tyson, entered the world on December 28, 2001, in the vibrant city of Brooklyn, New York.

While Devyn’s musical journey has gained attention and praise, she remains private about her personal life, including details about her parents.

However, we did discover that her mother’s name is Natasha Tyson and that her parents are from Trinidad.

Career In Music

Devyn’s journey in the music industry began at the age of 8, when she started improving her skills as a singer, composer, and songwriter, particularly in the genre of Soca music.

Her dedication and talent quickly caught the attention of audiences, and at the age of 13, she performed her first concert at the St. Stephen’s Lutheran Church auditorium in Brooklyn on November 21, 2015.

On November 26, 2019, Young Devyn released her debut single, titled “Nobody.” In interviews, she expressed her commitment to creating music without the use of profanity.

She attributed this approach to two significant factors. Firstly, she started writing music at a very young age, and profanity simply didn’t align with her expression at that time.

Secondly, her love for reading instilled in her a passion for expanding her vocabulary. When her grandma ran out of books for her to read, she was encouraged to explore the dictionary, which deepened her understanding of language and enriched her lyrical capabilities.

Devyn’s devotion to her music resulted in the release of her first EP, titled “Baby Goat,” on June 18, 2021.
