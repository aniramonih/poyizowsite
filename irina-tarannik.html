The Russian actress Irina Tarannik is married to Denis Vasilev.

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Irina Tarannik is a Russian actress, who is better known for her role as Svetlana Alexeyevna Toropova in the 2018-2019 science-fiction series Better Than Us. Tarannik has starred in various other plays, movies, and TV shows. From which she is grabbing constant media attention as a talented actress. 

The adorable actress Irina was born on October 12, 1985, in Blagoveshchensk, Russia, under the star sign of Libra. She is the daughter of Galina Panteleeva (mother) and her husband. Concerning education, Irina graduated from Mikhail Shchepkin Higher Theatre School, located in Moscow, Russia.

Is Irina Tarannik A Married Woman?

The 35-years-old actress Irina is in a marital relationship with Denis Vasilev. Tarannik's beloved husband, Vasilev, is also a Russian actor. Denis has starred in movies and TV series like Nostalgiya Po Budushemu, Sunstroke, Twist of Fate, The Expendables 2, and Y.O.P. 

Like Irina, Denis also attended Mikhail Shchepkin Higher Theatre School. Vasilev and Tarannik got married in July 2014 after staying in a romantic relationship for several months. 

The actor Denis and the actress Irina tied the wedding knot at Yacht Club Aurora situated in Dolgoprudnyy, Moscow, Russia. On the big day, the beautiful bride wore a lace wedding gown that features a long veil. 

On the other hand, the groom Denis dashes the wedding look wearing a black tuxedo, a white shirt, and a white tie. As of now, the husband-wife duo is sharing a healthy bond as a perfect married partner. Additionally, they are also parenting their kids jointly. 

Shared An Adorable Daughter

Following the strong marital bond, Irina and her husband Denis shared a beautiful daughter Tayusha Vasilev in October 2015. 

As of now, the five-year-old Tayusha is living happily with her beloved and celebrated parents. She is also grabbing media attention as a celebrity kid. The Vasilev family of three shares a good bond. They visit many exotic places together. And Irina also never misses a chance to share pictures with her hubby and daughter on her Instagram account.

Career Highlights

After graduating from art school, Irina works hard to pursue a better career as an actress. She is a member of the Russian Academic Youth Theater. Irina has played in various theatrical shows. 

Similarly, Tarannik has portrayed her character in the Russian TV mini-series like the 2011 Byla Tebe Lyubimaya (English: Was Your Favorite), Stroyka (2013), 2016 Lyubov kak Stikhiynoe Bedstvie (Loves as a Natural Disaster), 2019 Bud, Chto Budet, and as Larisa in Nezabytaya (Unforgettable).

Likewise, Irina's movie credit includes. Her role as Olga in Rain In May, in Ya Dumal, ty Budesh Vsegda (I Thought You Would Last Forever), A Picture To Remember, Kaleydoskop Sudby (Kaleidoscope of Fate), and others. 

Furthermore, actress Irina is also known for portraying her role in the series Better Than Us, where another Russian actress Paulina Andreeva has also played a lead role.
