Are Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright Getting Divorced? Is Their Separation Rumors True?

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Recently, there have been reports that Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright are divorcing, so Vanderpump Rules viewers decided to investigate.

Many people are still interested in the couple, even if they are no longer on the show. The pair has had its ups and downs, but they welcomed a kid together more than a year ago.

First, like with his previous partners, Jax cheated on Brittany, and their friends urged her to leave him. The two decide to wed instead, but can their union last?

Jax Taylor and Brittany Cartwright declared they would not return to Vanderpump Rules in December 2020.

“The last 5 years on Vanderpump Rules have been some of the most challenging, rewarding and fulfilling years of my life,” Brittany wrote on Instagram at the time. “Although this is difficult to share, Jax and I will not be returning for another season of Vanderpump Rules.”

She added, “We are excited to take this time to focus on our growing family and share with you our new endeavors.”

At first glance, there was no wrong turn of events between the network and the pair.

No “bad blood” existed between the network and the pair, claims TMZ. Instead, the couple split up because they thought the show’s direction didn’t fit their new family lifestyle.

Since then, Jax and Brittany have been busy raising Cauchi, the couple’s little son, but some fans began to worry that their union may end up like Tom Schwartz and Katie Maloney’s.

On November 18, Reddit member @ItsNotAllHappening expressed their hypothesis that the pair is on their way to splitsville.

“It seems like something is going on between these two. Has anyone noticed this?” they wrote. “While she was in KY w/Cruz for over a week, he stayed back and said ‘he needed a break from them’ on an IG live… He hasn’t liked any of her pics on IG lately. Not solo pics of her or even her video of Cruz. She also has not liked or commented on his since August.”

In the comments, many VPR fans thought this was coming long.

“For me the tell was when Brittany took off for Vegas without notice. I knew something was up then,” one fan commented on the post. “She’s not my favorite but I love this for her. That man never really loved her. I don’t know that she can do better, but I’m sure she can’t do worse.”

“100% something is up! I’ve been following this on the snark sub as well,” another wrote. “Biggest red flag right now is she apparently just got back from Kentucky and he has yet to post about it, specifically Cruz. It’s fishy for sure!”

“My friend saw him alone at Tomtom the other night which seems strange. I would think he’d be too resentful to go there,” a third added. “Only reason would to be for ‘fans.'”

“To this day I cannot believe she married him,” a fan said, siding with Brittany. “After everything he did to try to make her leave, she went through with it. And then tied herself to him for life through that beautiful child. Love yourself better in the years to come, Brit.”

But now, Jax is publicly defending his marriage.

According to The Hollywood Gossip, a fan allegedly commented on a picture of the couple wondering whether they were breaking up.

“You two look incredible together! Please tell me the divorce rumors are false! You belong together [red heart emoji],” they wrote.

“We would never get divorced, we don’t believe in that. Not sure [why] people are saying that,” Jax replied.

Jax’s response was met with skepticism from the outlet, mainly because he chose to defend the marriage contract rather than his wife’s love.
