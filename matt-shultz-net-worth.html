Matt Shultz Biography

Matt Shultz, born October 23, 1983, in Bowling Green, KY. Lead singer and guitarist for Cage the Elephant, an alternative rock band known for songs like “Ain't No Rest for the Wicked” and “In One Eye.” He is inspired by punk bands including The Pixies, which featured Kelley Deal on bass and vocals. He grew up in Bowling Green, Kentucky, creating Cage the Elephant with some of his high school friends.
