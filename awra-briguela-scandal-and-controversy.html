Awra Briguela Scandal And Controversy: Why Was She Arrested?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 16, 2024

Awra Briguela Scandal has made headlines in many online sources. Learn the truth about the scandal involving Awra Briguela that made headlines and the cause of her arrest.

Awra Briguela is a young Filipino comedian and actor. She rose to prominence after being hand-picked for the character of “MakMak” in FPJ’s Ang Probinsyano (2016–present) by the show’s star, Coco Martin.

In 2016, the title episode of Maalaala Mo Kaya featured Briguela’s life. She has crowned the winner of the first season of Your Face Sounds Familiar: Kids in the Philippines in 2017.

Briguela enjoys playing the parts of gorgeous Filipina actors and actresses like Maja Salvador, Sarah Geronimo, and Sarah Lahbati. 

She gained popularity under the alias “Awra” after breaking out on social media.

Awra Briguela Scandal And Controversy

Awra Briguela is caught up in a scandal and a dispute that started on June 29.

The 19-year-old starlet is seen being held by police outside a bar in Makati City in a social media video.

Awra allegedly confronted a group of men and demanded that one of them remove his clothes, according to the Southern Police District (SPD).

A violent confrontation ensued when his request was turned down, and she tore his shirt.

Several well-known internet users and celebrities have endorsed Awra. Notably, Awra’s longtime friend and YouTuber Riva Quenery spoke out in support of her.

Riva demanded more research be done into whether any other males were involved in the brawl and questioned why Awra was the only one seized and detained.

Following the event, Briguela’s friend and fellow content producer Zayla Nakajima rushed to social media to disprove the claims spreading.

Zayla published her account, claiming that Awra’s actions were driven by the urge to defend their friends from men who were reportedly attempting to engage in sexual harassment, even though her post has since been removed.

This explanation was Zayla’s attempt to present a different viewpoint.

These recent events have sparked a lot of discussion and call for a closer examination of the facts behind the Briguela issue and subsequent arrest.

People eagerly anticipate more details and clarification as the situation develops to help explain the unfolding events.

Why Was Awra Briguela Arrested?

An altercation outside a bar in Makati City led to the arrest of Briguela, a well-known Filipino comedian.

Charges of physical harm, shock and scandal, disobedience of the authorities, and blatant assault were brought against her due to the incident.

Awra allegedly caused a disturbance at Bolt Hole Bar, according to the Southern Authorities District (SPD), and was caught by authorities on Thursday at about 5 am.

According to complainant Mark Christian Ravana, the incident allegedly occurred at a farewell party.

Awra and several other individuals allegedly confronted Ravana’s group after approaching them. The situation worsened as Awra and her pals joined her in tearing Ravana’s shirt brutally.

Moreover, Awra is accused of being verbally aggressive with the police when they interfered because she disregarded their authority. Additionally, she shoved one of the arresting police officers.

Different versions and opinions added to the ongoing conversation about Briguela’s detention and the specifics of the altercation after the incident caused a flurry of discussion.

The city prosecutor’s office will now receive the case for further inquiry by the legal system.

As the investigation goes on, many reports and viewpoints have surfaced, adding to the ongoing debate about Briguela’s detention and the circumstances of the incident.

Briguela is still in custody and will remain there until the investigation ends.
