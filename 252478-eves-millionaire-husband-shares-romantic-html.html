Eve's Millionaire Husband Maximillion Cooper Shares Romantic Photo with Her on Valentine's Day

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 29, 2024

Star rapper Eve received a warm Valentine's Day greeting from her spouse on social media as he shared one of their sweet moments while reveling in the celebration of love.

Eve's husband Maximillion Cooper dedicated a heartwarming Instagram post to her as they joined in the Valentine's Day celebration. The business mogul chipped in a sweet message to Mrs Cooper.

Cooper attached a sweet photo of him and his wife to the hearty message. The snapshot seemed to have been taken during one of the couple's vacations as they warmed up to each other for a selfie.

Eve and Maximillion Cooper arrive in a Maserati at "Fashion For Relief" at The British Museum on September 14, 2019. | Photo: Getty Images

The pair took the photo while on a boat expenditure where they donned protective clothes, matching their glams with dark shades. The doting husband addressed his long time wife thus:

"Happy Valentine's Day Mrs Cooper."

Eve and Cooper have been together for closer to a decade, and while they faced criticism for their interracial marriage, fans have witnessed them grow together through the years.

Cooper and the award-winning rapper got married in 2014, and according to Eve, she and her husband have kept their love life going because of the respect they have for each other.

She referred to their relationship as the most real she has ever had, and presently, they are working towards expanding their family. Eve has never been a mom, but she has had some experiences with Cooper's kids from his older marriage.

Last December, the "Tambourine" rapper opened up to People about her family, and how they have fared amid quarantine. She noted that being home with her husband has solidified their union, adding:

"Honestly, it's been really good. I have to say, I feel lucky that we're one of the couples that got out of the quarantine and was like, 'I still like you'..."

The star shared that she realized that being with her man was a great choice, and the quarantine helped them learn new things about each other. Once in an interview, Eve admitted that being married changed her a lot, and it was a welcoming change.

The couple first met in 2010 at a Gumball 3000 event and tied the knot in an Ibiza ceremony four years later. Once speaking about their marriage, Cooper stated that being with Eve was an amazing experience.

The star rapper fell in love with Cooper and his kids, but it did take some time. Eve noted that she wasn't sure about being a stepmom but all that changed soon as she became accustomed to Cooper's kids.
