Pape’s participation at the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar

Senegal qualified for the FIFA World Cup 2022 by finishing fifth in the third round of CAF matches. Senegal’s national team will be represented by 26 men at the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar. When Senegal’s national team manager, Aliou Cisse, named the 26-man roster, Pape was included. As a result, he is representing Senegal at the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar. He now plays as a midfielder for Senegal’s national team.

How did he begin his professional career and qualify for the FIFA World Cup 2022?

Pape began his professional football career with the team Generation Foot. After a few years, he signed a five-year deal with Ligue 1 team Metz on September 15, 2020. He played for Metz’s second team shortly after joining the club. However, after seeing his outstanding performance, the manager promptly summoned him up to the first squad. However, he was unable to participate in the season since it was interrupted due to the coronavirus outbreak.

On November 29, 2020, he made his first-team debut in a Ligue 1 match. Pape signed a deal with English Premier League team Tottenham Hotspur on August 27, 2021. However, he was loaned back to Metz for one season. Aliou Cisse, the manager of Senegal’s national team, called him to participate in the AFCON 2021 qualifier on March 26, 2021. While he was on the squad, they won the 2021 Africa Cup of Nations. He performed well in this competition. As a result, he was chosen by the management to play in the FIFA World Cup 2022 in Qatar.
