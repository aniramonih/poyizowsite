What Happened To Kim G From The Real Housewives Of New Jersey?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Real Housewives are always elite socialites, but Kim Granatell takes it to the next level. According to The Daily Dish, Kim's life only got more dramatic after leaving the show in 2011. Her husband apparently threatened to divorce her unless she quit the series, and as Kim somewhat joked to Celebrity Magnet in 2012, he divorced her anyway. She also decided to back out of competing in a Mrs. New Jersey pageant that same year, per NJ.com. 

Kim was quickly linked to Lindsay Lohan's father Michael Lohan following her divorce, according to Starcasm. She even boasted to Page Six that Michael was a "great guy" and "affectionate lover." 

Kim was also connected with former RHONJ co-star Danielle Staub's ex-fianc√©, Joe Masalta, on Twitter, per Reality Tea. She offered to pay his court fees in a lawsuit against Danielle. Kim also claimed in January 2020 via a since-deleted Twitter post that Danielle was really fired from the show after getting physical with fellow Housewife Margaret Josephs, per The Sun. 

Seems like her RHONJ grudges run deep! Maybe Kim really has not moved on from reality TV after all.
