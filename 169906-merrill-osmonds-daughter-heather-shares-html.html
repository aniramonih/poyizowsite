Merrill Osmond's Daughter Heather Shares Photo of Her Children Alexis, Paige, Landon and Jocelyn

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 12, 2024

Merrill Osmond's daughter, Heather Osmond takes picture with all her children. She admits that this is the first family picture they have taken one in years.

Related to the famous Osmond family, Heather Osmond is a former beauty queen. In 2007 she he won Miss Teen Utah and went on to enter the national competition making it to the top 15 in Miss America.

Coming from humble beginnings, Osmond began her career as a real estate agent. Her love for designing homes led her to establish Osmond designs.

Married to Jared Osmond, she is the proud mother of four children. Sharing a picture of her family she admits that is has been long time since they have taken a family picture, saying:

"When I was first married, my cute mom gave me great advice that I still appreciate and follow. She said...Invest in family photos. But at times, taking yearly family photos were a big sacrifice."

Appreciating precious family moments Osmond goes on to say:

"However, looking back it was one of the greatest investments because those memories are absolutely priceless to me."

Trying to juggle her teenage children’s lives she admits:

"It’s been almost 2 years since we’ve had family photos taken (because Alexis served a 1 1/2 year LDS mission in Scotland and Ireland) and this is one of the backstage photos I took today of my amazing kids. I can’t wait to see the rest."

Sharing a behind the scenes photo, Osmond holds a camera and she takes pictures of her teenage children standing together as they flash big smiles for their mother.

Speaking on the importance of embracing every moment with her children, Osmond tells Mommy High Five:

"Love your kids. Stay close to your kids. They all are different and need different things. Stay close to your mom intuition and it will help you along your way."

Osmond has managed to maintain a close relationship with her children, from dance recitals, to checking up with her children on video calls while they are doing missionary work, she is a hardworking and hands on mom.
