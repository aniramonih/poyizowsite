Is Priscilla Presley Still Alive or Dead?

Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 25, 2024

Priscilla Ann Presley is an American businesswoman and actress. She is also the former wife of American singer Elvis Presley. She is known for starring in Naked Gun films and on the long-running television series Dallas. She was born on May 24, 1945, at Brooklyn Naval Hospital in Brooklyn.

Priscilla met Elvis in Bad Nauheim, Germany in 1959 when she was just 14. She made quite an impression on Elvis and they were married in 1967. In 1968 their daughter, Lisa-Marie was born.

Priscilla and Elvis’ marriage was rocky, riddled with infidelity on both parties. By 1972, Priscilla met Mike Stone, a karate instructor, and had an affair with him which caused her divorce from Elvis.

After her divorce, Priscilla ventured into several businesses including opening a boutique that closed in 1976.

She became the executor of Elvis’s will after he died. She opened Graceland for tourism and this paid up greatly and yielded over $100 million over the years.

Fig 1. Priscilla Presley, her husband Elvis, and their daughter Lisa-Marie

Priscilla was linked to photographer Terry O’Neill, lawyer Robert Kardashian, hairdresser Elie Ezerzer and financier Kirk Kerkorian. She lived with Marco Antonio Garcia and she gave birth to their son, Navarone Garibaldi in 1987.

Is Priscilla Presley Still Alive or Dead?

Priscilla Presley is alive and still very active in managing Graceland. In 2019 she appeared in the documentary, Wedding at Graceland.
