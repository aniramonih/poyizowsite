Landon Collins family, wife, children, parents, siblings

Posted by Kelle Repass on Thursday, August 29, 2024

NFL safety and linebacker Landon Alexander Collins was born on January 10, 1994. He plays for the New York Giants (NFL). At Alabama, where he played collegiate football, he was a member of the squad that prevailed in the 2013 BCS National Championship Game. During the 2015 NFL Draft’s second round, the New York Giants selected him. During his time with them, Collins was selected for the 2016 All-Pro Team and participated in three Pro Bowls. The Washington Redskins football team is another team for which he has played.

Collins, a native of New Orleans, Louisiana, spent his formative years in the Algiers district. Collins and his family had to evacuate the city in the wake of Hurricane Katrina in 2005, and they eventually made their way to Geismar in Ascension Parish.

Tom Collins, Landon’s father, coached him in football from the age of 4 to 12. The most important coach I’ve ever had, according to Landon, is his father.

Collins stated that his father gave his son the nickname “Money” while coaching him because at the age of six, anything he touched was just gold.

Parents and Siblings

He is the son of April Justin and Thomas Collins and was raised woth two siblings; Gerald Willis (brother) and Gerrah Willis (sister).

Spouse and Children

He is engaged to Victoria Lowrey, his college sweetheart but they don’t have any kids together.
