Demetrious Johnson’s Career

Demetrious Johnson’s career in mixed martial arts began in 2007. He quickly rose through the ranks, earning a reputation for his technical skills and unmatched speed. Transition to ONE Championship

In 2018, Johnson made a surprising move by leaving UFC and joining ONE Championship, a Singapore-based promotion. This move was part of a historic “trade” between the two promotions, which also involved Ben Askren moving to UFC. Despite the change in promotion, Johnson’s earning potential remained high due to his status as one of the world’s top fighters.
