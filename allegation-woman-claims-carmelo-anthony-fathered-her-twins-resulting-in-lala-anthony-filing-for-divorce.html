Allegation: Woman Claims Carmelo Anthony Fathered Her Twins, Resulting in Lala Anthony Filing for Di

Posted by Martina Birk on Friday, August 9, 2024

A woman has made a claim that Carmelo Anthony is the father of her newborn twins, allegedly leading to Lala Anthony filing for divorce.

The woman stated that she met Carmelo in New York in 2020, and after initially rejecting his advances, they developed a friendly relationship, which eventually turned sexual despite Carmelo being reconciled with his wife, Lala, and quarantining with her.

She mentioned that Carmelo portrayed himself as a single man, frequently FaceTiming her from his bed at home, shared with LaLa, and even not spending holidays with his family.

The woman disclosed that she discovered her pregnancy in September and gave birth prematurely, with one of the twins facing significant health issues.

She expressed her desire for Carmelo to be involved in their lives, as he has been difficult to reach since the birth, and emphasized that provision of a place and some financial support is inadequate for her children’s needs.

She acknowledged her mistake in the situation and expressed remorse, emphasizing her primary concern for her children’s well-being.

Furthermore, she indicated willingness to communicate with Lala but had doubts about being given the opportunity to do so.

The woman also shared screenshots of her conversations with Carmelo with Hollywood Unlocked.

Carmelo and LaLa had split in 2017 amid allegations of Carmelo fathering a child with a stripper, but they reunited in 2019, maintaining a cordial relationship for the sake of their 14-year-old son. However, Lala filed for divorce on Thursday, June 17, after 11 years of marriage.

The couple shares a 14-year-old son.
