Kubota MX5400 Utility Tractor is equipped with a Kubota V2403CR-T turbocharged diesel 4-cylinder liquid-cooled engine, which delivers 53 hp (39.5 kW) power at a rated rpm of 2700.

Kubota MX5400 Utility Tractor featured a Kubota 2.4L 4-cyl diesel engine, 11.9 gals 45.0 L fuel tank capacity, 4×2 2WD/4×4 MFWD 4WD, power steering, mechanical wet disc brakes, 2310 lbs rear lift, 3474 to 4268 pounds weight, 8 forward and reverse gears.

Kubota MX5400 Utility tractor attachments include a front-end loader and backhoe.
