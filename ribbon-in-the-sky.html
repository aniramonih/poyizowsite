Overall Meaning

The lyrics of "Ribbon In The Sky" by Rod Wave tell a story about the artist's feeling of isolation and pain, which he tries to alleviate by switching up his location to find solace. The opening lines depict the artist riding in his luxurious 500 Benz with no love for the people surrounding him since they are deceitful. He then expresses his disappointment in love by saying that nothing is what it seems to be, and he needs some happiness in his life. The only way for him to relieve this pain is to travel and clear his head.


The artist also talks about his struggles with his past and how he has to stay focused to achieve his goals since music is all he has ever wanted. He also mentions the need to be careful in the streets and the importance of having loyal friends. In the chorus, he talks about keeping his head to the ribbon in the sky, which is symbolic in the song, signifying the hope that things will get better, and he will find peace. The song ends with the artist asking for his mother's comfort, stating that he needs to talk to her as he has been struggling with his demons.


Overall, "Ribbon In The Sky" is about an artist's struggle with life, his mental health, and the hope for a better tomorrow.
