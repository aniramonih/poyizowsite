Sienna Miller Holds Hands with Archie Keswick in New Photos!

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 8, 2024

Sienna Miller might have a new guy in her life!

The 39-year-old actress was spotted holding hands with Archie Keswick while stepping out for a lunch date on Tuesday afternoon (June 1) in New York City.

Archie is a director at the Mandarin Oriental Hotel Group and he’s said to be a “longtime pal” of Sienna. In an article from over 10 years ago, The Guardian called him a “close friend” of the actress.

While no details about their relationship are known as of now, Sienna and Archie were seen holding hands during their walk around the city and they sat side-by-side at lunch rather than across the table from each other.

Last year, Sienna split from Lucas Zwirner, who was rumored to be her fiance.
