Nick Benedict Ex-Wife: Meet Michelle Dow

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Nick Benedict Ex-Wife: Meet Michelle Dow: Born on July 14, 1947, Nick Benedict was an American actor.

He developed a passion for acting at a younger age and made his acting debut in 1955 in the television series; Wiretapper.

Benedict was consistent throughout his career and rose to become one of the most sought-after American actors.

He was best known for his roles on daytime soap operas and was a key part of major soaps including; All My Children, Days of Our Lives, and The Young and the Restless.

Benedict played Phil Brent in All My Children, and Michael Scott in Young and the Restless, before playing Curtis Reed in Days of Our Lives, appearing in 93 episodes from 1993 to 2001.

He also appeared in 30 episodes of Tribes and 7 episodes of Santa Barbara. Apart from his soap opera roles, Benedict appeared in the original Mission Impossible and Hawaii Five-O series.

Sadly, the renowned actor has died. Nick Benedict passed away following complications from spinal surgery on Friday, July 14, 2023, his 76th birthday.

Before his death, a fundraiser was put in place that also explained how Benedict was undergoing spinal cord surgery and was being admitted to a hospice.

“Many of you know Jake’s Steakhouse bartender Ginger. She has been with us for many years. She has taken a leave of absence from us to handle an extreme medical issue with her husband Nick.

Nick Benedict, was in the Navy, an actor, musician and painter. Ginger and Nick have been married for 22 years. Both have been part of the Tehachapi community for many years.

On July 2nd, Sunday Nick had emergency spinal cord surgery. On July 4th he was admitted to hospice. He is paralyzed from the neck down. He will need constant care and support.

They live in Arizona and Ginger travels back and forth. To say this is a stressful situation is not saying enough. We are looking to raise money to help with bills, food and any medical expenses that may arise. Please pray for Ginger and Nick as they go through this very difficult time”

Benedict’s death was confirmed in an online obituary and in a statement put out by Jake’s Steakhouse, a California restaurant where his wife works as a bartender.

At the time of this report, the family was planning her final funeral arrangement and the details are yet to be finalized. We will keep you updated.

Nick Benedict Ex-Wife: Meet Michelle Dow

Nick Benedict married twice. His first marriage was with Michelle Dow. The pair tied the knot in 1976 and after a few months, they divorced the same year (1976).

Nick Benedict is survived by his wife, Ginger Loli-Benedict, who works as a bartender and manager at Jake’s Steakhouse, a restaurant in California.

It’s unknown when Benedict and Ginger married. However, they spent 22 years of married life together before his death.
