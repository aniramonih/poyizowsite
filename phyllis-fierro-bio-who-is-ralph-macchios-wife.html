Who is Phyllis Fierro? All About Ralph Macchio's Wife of Over 30 Years?

Posted by Billy Koelling on Monday, August 5, 2024

Phyllis Fierro is the wife of Ralph Macchio, the American actor who is popular for appearing in three different Karate Kid movies. Fierro and her husband met while they were in their teenage years and fell in love at first sight. Their love blossomed, so they married and have remained together to date with two offspring who are now adults.

How Old is Phyllis Fierro?

Phyllis Fierro is 63 years old in 2023. She was born in the United States of America on July 3rd, 1960. Not much is known about her except that she got married to a famous actor 10 years after their first meeting.  Phyllis has succeeded in living a life away from the limelight.

While her husband made a career in the movie industry, she went ahead to pursue a career as a medical worker. Ever since she got married to Ralph, she has maintained a low profile. It has been noticed that she is not active on any of the social media platforms. Perhaps her personal interests are known to her children and husband. Also, Phyllis has no issue with the glitz and glamour of the spotlight, but she is not interested in it.

Phyllis Fierro is of caucasian ethnicity and American nationality. She was born in the United States of America, which makes her an American citizen. The details of Fierro’s family background are still shrouded in mystery, and this makes it difficult to tell her exact ancestral heritage.

However, it is possible that Fierro is of European descent, possibly Spanish, Austrian, or Italian, going by her surname. Of course, her husband Ralph Macchio is of European descent as his father has Italian and Greek roots while his mother has Italian ancestry.

What Does Phyllis Fierro Do For a Living?

Phyllis Fierro is a nurse practitioner. She has a career in the medical industry as a practicing nurse and is noted as one of the heroic nurses who worked at the front lines during the COVID-19 crisis, a feat that has fetched her praise from her husband as well as her kids.

In March 2020, her daughter Julia took to Instagram to write, “My mom is a nurse practitioner … She’s my hero just by being my mom, and I know she has been (and will continue to be) a hero to so many through her unlimited compassion,”.

“Just wanted to express my deepest gratitude, not only for her, but for everyone working in healthcare. They teach us that kindness, dedication, selflessness, and empathy are superpowers.”

The wife of the Hollywood star has been able to establish herself in her profession. Her current net worth is an estimated $3 million. Nothing is known of her cars and houses due to her private life. However, it is a known fact that she lives in the same house with her husband in Long Island, New York.

When Did Ralph Macchio and Phyllis Fierro Meet?

Ralph Macchio and Phyllis Fierro met in 1975 in his grandmother’s basement. The duo met as teenagers when they were in high school during the 16th birthday party of one of Ralph’s cousins. At that time, Ralph was 15 years old. The actor noted that Phyllis was his cousin’s friend whom he fell in love with at first sight. Records have it that Ralph began taking dance classes at the age of 3 and started performing with a local dance crew before he clocked 16 years old. Therefore, he was relatively popular when he met Phyllis.

Ralph and Phyllis dated all through high school and tied their marital knot 10 years after their meeting. They were 15 and 16 years of age, respectively, when they first met; indeed, as teenagers, it couldn’t have been anything else but love at first sight. They moved from being teenage friends who were fond of each other to getting married on April 5, 1987, and have remained married for 36 years now.

Even after their wedding in 1987, the couple hasn’t just been together, but they seem to have fallen deeper in love with themselves after many years of being married. Phyllis Fierro and Ralph Macchio are one of the cutest and most admired couples in Hollywood. Perhaps we can accord this to the fact that only one person is in the entertainment world.

They Have Been Married For 36 Years Now

One of the things that becomes a problem in the life of some actors is that their marriage doesn’t last. However, Phyllis and Ralph have been able to remain married for a long time despite all the odds, including one of which Ralph described as himself being used to “one foot in, one foot out.” The couple who have been married for 36 years have found a way to make their love strong enough to withstand any hardship. In an interview with People TV, Ralph mentioned that his long-term partner has been more than supportive, especially in filling the gap when he is absent due to his job.

In their home, Phyllis has been the support system of her husband in creating a balance between his work life and being a family man. He noted that without Phyllis and without the foundation they had laid down and the family they established, it would have been difficult to maintain the one-foot-in, one-foot-out relationship he has in Hollywood. The actor states that without Phyllis, there would not have been a balance in their marriage. He credited both of them for their commitment to the marriage. He especially commended his wife for being by his side all the whole time.

Also, speaking about their commitment to each other, the actor disclosed that in both difficult and hard times, and through all they have encountered, he believes that they are meant to be together.

Phyllis and Raph Macchio are Parents To Two Children 

The union of the duo has been blessed with two children: a male and a female. The kids are named Julia Macchio and Daniel Macchio.
