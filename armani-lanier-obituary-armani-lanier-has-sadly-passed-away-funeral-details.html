Armani Lanier Obituary, Armani Lanier Has Sadly Passed Away, Funeral Details

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Armani Lanier Obituary, Death – Armani Michael Jonathan Lanier, a vibrant soul at the tender age of 3, left this world unexpectedly on October 6, 2023. Born on October 16, 2019, to Jonathan Lanier and Cassandra Schnabel, Armani brought immense joy to his family during his brief yet impactful journey. Armani’s zest for life was evident in his love for family and the simple pleasures of childhood. He cherished moments exploring the outdoors, fishing, riding his Spiderman Scooter, and playing at the park with his loved ones.

His infectious laughter and playful spirit created lasting memories for those fortunate enough to share in his adventures. He leaves behind a grieving yet loving family, including his parents, Jonny and Cassie, and his little brother, Josiah, residing in Oshkosh. Grandparents Nichole Schnabel, LaTonya and Marc Rivera, and Jonathon Lanier Sr., along with great-grandparents Linda Schnabel, Michelle Sneed, Kerri Cable, Tommie Spikes, and Dwayne Thomkins, mourn the loss of this precious child. Armani’s aunts, uncles, and cousins, along with his cherished pets Simba, Oats, and Honey, also feel the void left by his departure.

Armani is preceded in death by his Grandpa Shawn Sworske, and 2X Great Grandparents Audrey Sauer, Morell Sneed, Johnnie L Thomkins, and Uncle Derek (Romeo) Spikes. The family invites friends and relatives to join them in remembering Armani during a Gathering on Tuesday, October 17, 2023, from 11:00 AM to 12:45 PM at the Phillip Funeral Home Chapel in West Bend, WI. A Funeral Service will follow at 1:00 PM, offering an opportunity to celebrate and honor the beautiful, albeit brief, life of Armani Michael Jonathan Lanier.
