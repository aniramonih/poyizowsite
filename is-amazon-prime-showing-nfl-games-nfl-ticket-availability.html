Is Amazon Prime Showing NFL Games? All about 2023-24 NFL Sunday Ticket Availability

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Amazon Prime Video has recently entered into an agreement to become the streaming platform, for broadcasting Thursday Night Football games. This collaboration with the NFL has brought the thrill of football action to Amazon Prime subscribers establishing it as a choice for anyone interested, in watching the latest NFL matchups.

If you’re a member of Amazon Prime watching Thursday Night Football games is easy. All you have to do is log in to your Amazon Prime Video account. You’ll see these games ready, for streaming. This special feature lets you experience the excitement of Thursday Night Football from the comfort of your home.

What Other NFL Games Are Available on Prime Video?

Although Amazon Prime Video doesn’t have access, to all NFL games because of the agreement between the NFL and DirecTV for NFL Sunday Ticket until 2033 it still provides NFL content. Apart from Thursday Night Football, you can also watch NFL RedZone, a channel that gives you updates on scoring from all NFL games. This way you can stay informed, about the touchdowns, field goals, and game-changing plays as they unfold throughout the league.

All About NFL Sunday Ticket Availability

What Is NFL Sunday Ticket?

NFL Sunday Ticket is a premium service that caters to die-hard NFL fans who want to watch games that aren’t locally broadcast in their area. It’s the ultimate solution for football enthusiasts who don’t want to miss a single snap of the action, especially when their favorite teams are playing outside their local TV market.

How Much Does NFL Sunday Ticket Cost?

The price of the NFL Sunday Ticket can differ based on the provider you choose. For example, DirecTV offers this service at a cost of $395, per season granting you access, to a selection of out-of-market NFL games.

Where Can I Buy an NFL Sunday Ticket?

To purchase an NFL Sunday Ticket subscription, you have several options. While DirecTV has traditionally been a popular choice, streaming services like YouTube TV and Hulu + Live TV have entered the scene, offering more flexible and diverse pricing options. These streaming platforms provide the convenience of accessing NFL Sunday Ticket content on various devices, giving you the freedom to choose the option that best suits your preferences.

Is NFL Sunday Ticket Available on Amazon Prime Video?

At the moment you won’t find NFL Sunday Ticket, on Amazon Prime Video because DirecTV holds the rights to it. However don’t worry, as there are still ways to catch NFL games on Amazon Prime Video. By tuning in to Thursday Night Football and using NFL RedZone, for scoring updates, you can still experience all the thrills of the NFL through Amazon Prime Video.
