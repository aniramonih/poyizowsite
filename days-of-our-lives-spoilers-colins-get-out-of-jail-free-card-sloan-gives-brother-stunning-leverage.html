Days of Our Lives Spoilers: Colins Get-Out-of-Jail-Free Card Sloan Gives Brother Stunning Lev

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 14, 2024

Days of Our Lives (DOOL) spoilers reveal that Colin Bedford (Jasper Newman) will meet with his attorney, who also happens to be his sister. Sloan Petersen (Jessica Serfaty) keeps trying to convince Colin to plead guilty so she can work out a deal for him, but he’s determined to fight the charges against him.

Although Sloan feels certain her brother’s going to do serious prison time, Colin doesn’t want to accept that outcome.

As a result, Colin may latch onto any possible strategy that might restore his freedom.

Sloan knows that Colin has done some truly awful things, but she still loves him.

Colin is the only family Sloan has left, so she sees him differently and trusts him more than the rest of Salem right now.

It’ll be easy for Sloan to get lulled into a false sense of security during the week of June 26-30.

Days spoilers say Sloan will open up to Colin during their attorney-client meeting, so that’s when she’ll make a confession.

It’s possible Sloan will simply confess she wants to have a baby with Eric Brady (Greg Vaughan), but we should also consider the possibility that she’ll take things a step further.

Could Sloan unburden herself and admit she rigged Nicole Walker’s (Arianne Zucker) DNA test?

Since Days spoilers are acting like this confession is a big deal, Sloan might come clean about her own transgression.

Perhaps Sloan will focus on the brother she loves instead of the criminal who could use this news to his advantage.

Sloan may not expect Colin to turn on her, but he absolutely could!

Since Colin is so adamant about avoiding prison, he might back Sloan into a corner to get what he wants.

If Colin learns Sloan’s stunning secret, he’ll also gain some leverage he can use for an ultimatum.

Maybe Colin will warn Sloan to make sure he gets off the hook or else he’ll tell Nicole, Eric and anyone else who’ll listen about her paternity trick.

Colin could point out that Sloan will lose Eric forever if he finds out she swabbed her own cheek for the test.

This would be a good opportunity to show Sloan the kind of person Colin really is since she doesn’t seem to fully understand.

It would also keep Colin involved in a DOOL storyline, which is something Days writers seem keen on doing since he’s not offscreen yet.

Sloan might be faced with the impossible task of clearing Colin’s name or getting this case thrown out somehow.

Although Sloan may have no idea how to pull that off, Colin could push her to figure it out or risk having her world blown up.

Days of Our Lives spoilers say Sloan will give Colin some shocking updates, but she may regret it in the aftermath!
