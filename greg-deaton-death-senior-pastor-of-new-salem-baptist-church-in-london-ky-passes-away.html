Greg Deaton Death, Senior Pastor of New Salem Baptist Church in London, KY, Passes Away

Posted by Patria Henriques on Monday, August 12, 2024

The London, KY community is mourning the sudden and tragic loss of Greg Deaton, the Senior Pastor of New Salem Baptist Church. Greg’s passing has left a void in the hearts of many, as he touched the lives of countless individuals with his leadership and compassion. Let’s delve into the details surrounding this heartbreaking event and the impact it has had on the community.

About Greg Deaton

Greg Deaton served as the Senior Pastor of New Salem Baptist Church in London, KY, for a significant period of time. He was widely respected for his devotion to his congregation and his unwavering commitment to spreading the message of faith and love. Greg’s kind-hearted nature and compassionate spirit endeared him to both his church community and the wider London area.

Greg Deaton Death

On Sunday, November 19, 2023, Greg Deaton passed away unexpectedly, leaving the community in shock and sadness. The details surrounding his passing have not been disclosed publicly. Impact on the New Salem Baptist Church Community

The New Salem Baptist Church community is grieving the loss of Pastor Greg Deaton, who played an integral role in their spiritual journey. As they mourn his passing, the church community will come together to honor his memory and continue his legacy of faith and compassion.

Support and Condolences

The news of Greg Deaton’s passing has reverberated beyond the walls of the New Salem Baptist Church. Conclusion

The London, KY community is grappling with the sudden loss of Greg Deaton, the Senior Pastor of New Salem Baptist Church.
