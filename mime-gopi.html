Timeline

Mime Gopi is an Indian stage and film actor who has appeared in Tamil films including Madras (2014), Kathakali (2016), and Kabali (2016).

Prior to his entrance into the film industry, Gopi was a renowned mime actor in Chennai and headed G Mime Studio, a studio which exhibited the art form. In his role, he worked closely with children with special needs to help them adapt miming skills. The actor celebrates every birthday and festivals by doing charity work across Tamil Nadu. He also had contributed money to the local festivals of the religious organisations. He is the inspiration to the upcoming theatre actors. He had set up the studio in 1994 and had worked to expand the art across Chennai. Gopi made a breakthrough as a film actor after appearing as a rogue in Madras (2014), and he arrived at the bang. He has since appeared as the antagonist in films such as Maari (2015) and Kathakali (2016). His upcoming movie Kavalthurai Ungal Nanban teaser got released. Times of India reviewed that the film carries a plot that has realistic touch and at the same time is very well presented with engaging elements by filmmaker RD and producer of this film stated "I was pretty confident that it has all elements to impress the crowds and didn’t want to miss the project".
