Know the Relationship Status of Famous Latino Journalist, Maria Hinojosa and her Earnings

Posted by Kelle Repass on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Emmy Award Winner Maria Hinojosa has been in the American Journalism industry for more than three decades. Maria is a famous American Journalist and an anchor. She is also the founder, president, and CEO of Futuro Media Group, which produces the show.

Maria Hinojosa was born on July 2, 1961, in Mexico City, Mexico. She is among the four children born to Raul Hinojosa and his wife, Berta Hinojosa. She has a sister and two brothers. Her father worked as a professor at the University of Chicago. Maria attended Barnard College, where she graduated magna cum laude with a degree in Latin American studies in 1985. 

Three Decades-Long Marriage Life

Mariana has been married for almost three decades. Maria is living a happy married life and her husband, German Perez, in the United States. The love birds exchanged their wedding vows in the early 1990s. 

The exact date of their wedding is still under investigation. Maria had a private wedding ceremony. Only a few members from both the families and some close friends were invited to the beautiful ceremony. They had been dating for some time before exchanging their wedding vows.

Both of them seem to be very much supportive of each other. There have been no rumors of the couple separating shortly. 

Mother of Two Kids

It is a matter of the fact that Maria is the mother of two beautiful kids. The love birds were blessed with their first child as a son, Raúl Ariel Jesus de Todos Los Santos Perez-Hinojosa, in 1996. The family of three was living a happy life.

In 1998, The duo welcomed a new member of the family. They had a daughter, María Yurema Guadalupe de Los Indios Perez-Hinojosa, on May 5, 1998. Maria is very much supportive of her children. Both of them are at their University and doing well.

Net Worth and Earnings

Maria Hinojosa has an estimated net worth of more than $2 Million. She credits most of her net worth to her successful career of working as a Journalist. Nikole Hannah-Jones is a famous American Pulitzer Prize winner with a net worth of $3 Million. 

Maria serves as the executive producer of America By the Numbers with Maria Hinojosa: Clarkston, Georgia, which premiered on PBS as a Need to Know Election 2012. She has been the host of the Latino USA for its entire 20-year run and currently serves as a executive producer. 

Career in Journalism

Hinojosa worked for CNN's New York City bureau for eight years. Hinojosa has written two books: Raising Raul: Adventures Raising Myself and My Son, a motherhood memoir, and Crews: Gang Members Talk with Maria Hinojosa. 

Hinojosa has won Several national and international honors and awards for her work. She has won four Emmy Awards, including one in 2002 for coverage of the September 11, 2001, terrorist attacks. In 2007, she was inducted into the "She Made It" Hall of Fame at the Paley Center for Media/Museum of Television and Radio.

Since 1995, she has been named three times one of the 100 Most Influential Hispanics by Hispanic Business magazine. In 2012, She was honored with a honorary Doctorate of Humane Letters from DePaul University.
