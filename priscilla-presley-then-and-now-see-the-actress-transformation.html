Priscilla Presley Then and Now: See the Actress' Transformation

Posted by Tandra Barner on Tuesday, August 27, 2024

There’s no doubt Priscilla Presley is one of the most iconic Hollywood stars of the ’80s and ’90s. Besides marrying Elvis Presley, The Naked Gun actress has made fans fall in love with her effortless beauty, trendy style and overall electrifying personality.

Priscilla divorced the late “King of Rock ‘n’ Roll” in 1973, four years before his death. The pair had one child together, daughter Lisa Marie Presley, whom they welcomed in 1968. Lisa Marie had four children of her own, Riley Keough and late son Benjamin Keough, with ex-husband Danny Keough and twins Harper Vivienne Ann Lockwood and Finley Aaron Love Lockwood, with her fourth ex-husband, Michael Lockwood. 

Priscilla also welcomed one son, Navarone Garibaldi, with her ex-boyfriend, Marco Garibaldi, in 1987. In February 2022, she attended her son’s wedding to Elisa Achilli at the Schloss Hunigen Hotel in Switzerland. She shared a sweet photo from the ceremony on Instagram. 

“They’ve been together for four years and we all have been waiting patiently for this day,” the proud mom told People after the nuptials. 

The businesswoman has always remained close to her family. She enjoys spending time with her kids and grandkids. 

“She loves being the person the girls come to for questions about life,” an insider exclusively told Closer in June 2019. “She can’t believe how fast they’re growing and how much they know already. It goes both ways — sometimes Priscilla needs them to answer questions about her cell phone and the internet. She just loves having them around.”

On top of being a mom of two and a doting grandmother to Lisa Marie’s kids, Priscilla has also taken on some iconic acting roles over the years. From 1983 to 1988, the New York native portrayed Jenna Wade in the soap opera Dallas. She appeared in more than 140 episodes of the series before joining The Naked Gun film trilogy. 

In August 2019, Priscilla signed on to co-produce an adult animated series about Elvis’ life called Agent King. The premiere for the first season of the Netflix series was set for some time in 2022. In an August 2021 interview with The Commercial Appeal, the former Elvis Presley Enterprises chairwoman shared why her ex-husband’s legacy is still so impactful more than four decades after his death. 

​​​​“Elvis has brought so many people together,” she said. “He was so caring, so considerate, sensitive, of course – it’s like the fans are like him … because they’ve been gracious, loyal, brought their families and keep coming back. It really is like one big huge family.”

In 2022, Priscilla shined during several red carpet events to promote the biographical musical film, Elvis. She attended the Met Gala with the cast in a sleek black gown. Afterward, she joined them at the 75th Cannes Film Festival in France in May 2022 and later at the Graceland premiere of the film in June 2022. While her incredible fashion moments left everyone speechless, the mom of two was just happy to see her late ex-husband’s story be told in a new light. 

“I’ve seen Elvis, the film. I watched the trailer over a dozen times. But the words I heard from my daughter on how much she loved the film, and that Riley will love it too when she sees it brought tears,” the Spin City actress wrote on Instagram. “I relived every moment in this film. It took me a few days to overcome the emotions as it did with Lisa.”
