Porsha Williams Celebrates Baby Shower With RHOA Costars: Pics

Posted by Billy Koelling on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Coming soon! Pregnant Porsha Williams celebrated her baby shower with her Real Housewives of Atlanta costars on Sunday, February 24.

While the bash had an all-white theme for guests’ dress code, the mom-to-be, 37, wore a tight red dress that hugged her baby bump and matched her fiancé, Dennis McKinley, who was in a red North Face jacket. She paired the bold long-sleeve number with sparkly hoop earrings.

The couple, who got engaged in October 2018, announced that they are expecting their first child one month earlier. Williams and McKinley then revealed that they’ve got a baby girl on the way, after the reality star accidentally hinted at the sex with an Instagram comment.

“Now I’m totally excited,” the Bravo personality said in an Instagram video at the time. “It’s official. PJ is here! It’s a girl.”
