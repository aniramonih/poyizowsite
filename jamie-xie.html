Bling Empire Star Jamie Xie Biography

Jamie Xie (born on November 9, 1997) is a 24 years old wealthy Chinese heiress who is a fashion model by profession. She is the child of the tech billionaire Ken Xie. The youngster enjoys a lavish life and lives in her luxury apartment in New York. Despite having a hyper-rich father, she bears her expenses.

In 2021 this Chinese beauty stepped into the spotlight after registering her presence in a Christine Chiu-produced Netflix reality show named “Bling Empire.” She managed to earn hundreds of thousands of fans with her personality and nature. In 2022, Xie again appeared in the new installment of the “Bling Empire,” got released on May 13. This time two new stars also joined the show making the show exciting and giving viewers some spicy episodes.

 

Childhood, Family, and Fortune

Reality show star Jamie Xie (age 24 years) took birth on November 9, 1997, in San Francisco Bay Area, California, America. As we told earlier, her father is Ken Xie, a Chinese-American businessman. Mr. Xie is the founder of Fortinet, NetScreen, and SIS (Systems Integration Solutions).

Jamie was born with a silver spoon in her mouth and her dad Ken always made sure to fulfill all the wishes of his daughter. During her high school days, she used to be an equestrian and participated in races across America. Because of her engagement in horse races, she managed her high school years with online classes.

When Jamie completed her schooling, her mother had told her that if she managed to get into Parsons, she would get her a Birkin. The young girl managed to get a place in Parson but later decided to study at her hometown at the Fashion Institute of Design and Merchandising.
