Andy Lassner

Posted by Patria Henriques on Friday, August 16, 2024

Fans of the Ellen DeGeneres Show will recognize Andy Lassner. He is the Emmy Award-winning executive producer of The Ellen Show, who appears on the show on occasion and has been victim to Ellen’s famed on-air pranks.

After prior experiences as a producer on the talk show The Rosie O’Donnell Show, the Colombian-American personality joined the show in 2003.

He also produced Sophia Grace & Rosie’s Royal Adventure (2014), a film starring two little kids who were frequent guests on Ellen’s show, Sophia Grace and Rosie McClelland.

Salary & Net Worth:

Andy Lassner’s net worth and salary, according to Celebrity Net Worth, are $12 million and $5 million, respectively.

Andy Lassner’s Married Life:

Andy Lassner has been married twice and is currently dating Lorie Lassner, his second wife.

(Photo: Andy Lassner’s Twitter)In his early twenties, he married for the first time. Andy began using it again at this time, after eight years of sobriety and having conquered major alcohol and other drug addiction.

“I’m grateful every day that I didn’t die of an overdose,” he says, reminiscing about the good old days.

He recalled taking his baby daughter, Erin, in a cab in the middle of the night to “score” narcotics while the baby’s mother was out of town during an interview with VARIETY on November 5th, 2019. The encounter was one of his “lowest points,” he said.

He finally came clean from his addiction after a year of adhering to his responsibilities in a sober house.

Children:

Andy has three children: Erin, 23, and Ethan and Ryan, 12-year-old twins (age, as of 5th November 2019).

Erin, his oldest daughter, is from his first marriage, while Lorie, his second wife, is the mother of his two sons.

Andy Lassner’s Bio: Family:

Andy Lassner was born in Bogota, Colombia, on December 30, 1966. In an Orthodox Jewish home on New York’s Upper East Side, he grew up in a loving and stable family.

Danièle and Jules Lassner are his parents. Andy’s father, Danièle, first met Jules when he came into a New York publishing business looking for books on South American politics. Andy’s mother, Danièle Gorlin, was among the young assistants that assisted him. Later, in 1963, they married.

Andy’s father, who served in the US Marine Corps during WWII, passed away in November 2015 at the age of 92. He was also a powerful businessman and a leader in the Jewish community.
