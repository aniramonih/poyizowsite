How To Get A Moon Stone In Pokemon Let's Go Pikachu &amp; Eevee

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024
Recommended Videos

In Pokemon Let’s Go Pikachu & Eevee, you need stones to evolve certain Pokemon. These range from Fire Stones and Leaf Stones to Moon Stones and their newly introduced Ice Stones, but where can you find Moon Stones? There’s only one guaranteed spawn point in the game, and you need multiple to evolve Jigglypuff, Clefairy, Nidorina, and Nidorino. To find various Pokemon Let’s Go Moon Stones, you want to go to Mt Moon and hope that lady luck is on your side. Here is how to get a Moon Stone in Pokemon Let’s Go.

Pokemon Let’s Go Moon Stone Location – Saffron City

Firstly, let’s cover the one place you’re guaranteed to find a Moon Stone in Pokemon Let’s Go: Saffron City. When you get to the central city, head to the Copycat Girl’s house. Inside, interact with the white cupboard, and you’ll find a Moon Stone inside. Don’t worry – she’s not bothered if you steal it.

How to get a Moon Stone in Pokemon Let’s Go – Moon Stone

Now to get more Moon Stones, you have to go to Mt Moon. What a surprise, right? The problem is that there are no guaranteed spawns. Moon Stones inside Mt Moon respawn once a day, so if you don’t get enough on your first visit, you’re going to need to come back multiple times.

To find the Moon Stone, look for the Pokeball items and the craters on the floor. Both can contain Moon Stones; if a crater has one, your Pokemon following you will notice it and prompt an interaction. They’re also more likely to spawn the deeper you go inside Mt Moon, so make sure you’re exploring everywhere possible whenever you go Moon Stone hunting. Unfortunately, you can’t go back into Mt Moon from Cerulean City! You’ll need to wait until you can get back to the Pewter City side.

While you’re around Mt Moon, make sure you grab a Charmander too!
