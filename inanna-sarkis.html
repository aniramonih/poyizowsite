YouTuber Turned Actress Inanna Sarkis Has A Baby With Her Husband Matthew Noszka

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 4, 2024

Inanna Sarkis is not a new name to YouTube surfers. With over 3.7 million subscribers, Sarkis is known for her short films, vines, and funny clips. 

Besides her YouTube career, she has also been active in the movie industry. Her first onscreen role was as Kayla in the TV series Are You Afraid of the Dark in 2000. 

Here are the details of her personal and professional life.  

Inanna Sarkis Is Married

Yes, folks, Inanna Sarkis is a happily married woman. She exchanged her marital vows with her longtime boyfriend Matthew Noszka in 2017 in an intimate wedding ceremony. 

As per sources, the couple started dating back in 2015. Their romantic relationship was exposed to the public after they posted their intimate photo on Instagram. 

Her boyfriend is an American model and actor known for his modeling project with Nike, Calvin Klein, Moncler, Hugo Boss, Versace, and so on; similar to popular personalities like Michael Jordan, Ysabel Jordan, Jennie Pegouskie, and others. 

With her husband Matthew Noszka, Sarkis welcomed an adorable daughter Nova Noszka on 21 September 2020. 

With love, she calls her daughter a little potato. 

In an interview with Vogue, Inanna Sarkis shared the joy of finding out about her pregnancy. She added: It was a roller coaster of emotions. Excitement mixed with anxiety and fear of whether we would be ready to be parents or not. After a lot of tears and deep conversation, we realized how perfect of time it really was to start a family.         

Amid their happily married life, some online sources claim that the couple is still not married as Inanna Sarkis has never mentioned Matthew Noszka as her husband. 

Inanna Sarkis Net Worth: $3 million 

The multitalented personality is estimated to have a net worth of $3 million as of 2021. She made her riches from her diverse career as an American YouTuber, social media personality, singer, director, and actress. 

Moreover, Inanna Sarkis also makes money from her self-titled YouTube channel. As per a reliable site Social Blade, from her YouTube channel, Sarkis’ estimated monthly earnings is in between $823 to $13.2 thousand. Similarly, her yearly income is around $9.9 thousand to $158.1 thousand. 

Besides that, the Youtuber adds a reasonable sum of money from her social media sponsorships and endorsements. 

Career Highlights

Moving unto her career, Inanna Sarkis launched her YouTube channel on 17 December 2006. She gained popularity from her short films. On her channel, she has uploaded several self-made movies and short films. Some of her popular films include Am I Perfect?, Strength, and Relationships, to name a few. 

Not to mention, Sarkis is also an actress, model, director, singer, editor, as well as scriptwriter. She has uploaded four music videos on her self-titled YouTube channel. 

Besides that, the YouTube personality has around 25 acting credits including movies and TV series After We Collide, After, A Killer Walks Amongst Us, The Left Right Game, Trish & Scott, Brew Brothers, and many others. 

Regarding her future projects, she is set to be appearing in films like Séance as Alice, All-Star Weekend as Stephanie, and Deported as Sarah releasing in 2021 and onwards.
