All Facts You Need To Know About Linda Fiorentino

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 13, 2024

Linda Fiorentino is known as a former American actress who gained popularity for starring in many films since her debut with a leading role in the coming of age drama film Vision Quest which was released in 1985.

She has appeared in movies like After Hours, Gotcha!, Jade, Men in Black, Dogma, and many others. In this post, we’ll throw light on her including her professional and personal life.

Linda Fiorentino biography

Clorinda Fiorentino was born on March 9th, 1985 into an Italian-American family in South Philadelphia, Pennsylvania, United States of America. She is the seventh child of her parent’s eight children and was raised in Philadelphia but later moved to the Turnersville section of Washington Township with her family which is in nearby South Jersey.

With regards to her education, Linda Fiorentino graduated from Washington Township High School in Sewell, New Jersey where she started performing in plays at Rosemont College in suburban Philadelphia. In 1980, she graduated with a Bachelor of Arts degree in political science.

Linda Fiorentino used to train at the circle in the Square Theater School in Manhattan while working as a bartender in a nightclub.
