What is George Teichner's Net Worth?

George Teichner is a retired celebrity accountant and businessman who has a net worth of $50 million. He is probably best known for being the father of Aviva Drescher, one of the cast members on the hit Bravo television series, The Real Housewives of New York City. George Teichner was born in 1935 and currently lives in Miami Beach, Florida. He graduated from CUNY, and Baruch College in 1964 with a BBA and worked as a celebrity accountant for clients like the Beatles and Woody Allen. In 2014, George surprised cast members and viewers of The Real Housewives of New York City by proposing to his never before seen girlfriend, Dana Lavette Cody, on camera. Cody is 50 years younger than Teichner and their relationship came with its share of controversy.

Prior to meeting Cody, the cast and viewers knew Teichner as a hypersexual man who loved the ladies. He was seen making passes and questionable remarks to cast mate Sonja Morgan in previous seasons and while the proposal to his new girlfriend, Cody, took everyone by surprise, the proposal and the engagement are genuine. The startling age gap between the fiancees had all the cast members weighing in. When it came to Drescher, however, she was understanding. She stated that The Museum of Sex was an obvious choice for her father's engagement party and said she believed that Cody and her father are truly in love.
