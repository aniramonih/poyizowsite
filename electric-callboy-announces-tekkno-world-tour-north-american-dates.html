Electric Callboy Announces TEKKNO World Tour North American Dates

Posted by Martina Birk on Thursday, August 29, 2024

After releasing their latest record, TEKKNO, last year to critical acclaim (plus hitting #1 on the German album charts!), Electric Callboy have finally announced their return to North America! The group will headline TEKKNO World Tour, produced by Live Nation, in August/September. Tickets go on sale starting Friday, February 10th at 10AM local time on Ticketmaster.com. 

Citi is the official card of the Electric Callboy TEKKNO World Tour. Citi cardmembers will have access to presale tickets beginning Tuesday, February 7th at 8AM PT//11AM ET until Thursday, February 9th at 10PM local time through the Citi Entertainment program. For complete presale details visit www.citientertainment.com

On March 24th, Electric Callboy will release TEKKNO (Tour Edition) via Century Media Records. Featuring the full TEKKNO album, plus 5 live tracks that were recorded on their sold-out 2022 European tour, TEKKNO (Tour Edition)can be pre-ordered now here: https://electriccallboy.lnk.to/TEKKNOtouredition – where the record is available in the following versions:

–Standard CD jewel-case

–Transparent light blue-lilac marbled LP

–Ultra clear-red splattered LP available from Bravado (band exclusive; limited to 1000 copies)

–Yellow-black marbled LP available from CM Distro (limited to 500 copies)

–Digital album
