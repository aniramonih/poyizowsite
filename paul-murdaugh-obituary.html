Paul Murdaugh Obituary: How Many People were There to Pay Their Respects?

Posted by Patria Henriques on Thursday, August 15, 2024

This 2023 Netflix original true crime series has been years in the making, but it finally arrives. One of South Carolina’s most prominent families, the Murdaughs, is at the center of a series of murders, cover-ups, and criminal probes in the true crime book Murdaugh Murders: A Southern Scandal.

The three-part docuseries delves into the intricate crimes surrounding the rich Southern family, from the terrible boating accident that killed 19-year-old Mallory Beach to the stunning double homicide of Paul Murdaugh and Margaret “Maggie” Murdaugh at the family’s hunting lodge.

On Thursday, February 23, 2018, Alex Murdaugh, 54, testified in his trial for two counts of murder. The deaths of Murdaugh’s wife and youngest son are probably familiar to everyone who has been following the scandal for any length of time. Continue reading to find out what occurred in the weeks following June 7, 2021.

Paul Murdaugh Obituary

On the evening of Monday, June 7, 2021, Paul Terry Murdaugh, 22, of Hampton, South Carolina, joined his mother, Margaret Branstetter Murdaugh (Maggie), in death.

Paul’s early years were spent exploring the great outdoors with his father, Alex, and brother, Buster. Junior year at USC, he and his friends and family regularly attended Gamecocks games. It was rare for Paul to encounter an unfamiliar face, and he had many close companions. He was the type of person who would jump at the chance to help a stranger. Paul’s one-of-a-kind character stemmed from the fact that he was the most sincere and selfless person anyone had ever met.

The funeral for Paul and Maggie will be placed in the Hampton Cemetery on Friday, June 11, 2021, at noon. The funeral arrangements are being handled by Peeples-Rhoden Funeral Home in Hampton.

The Funeral for Paul and Maggie Murdaugh was held a few days After their Killings

On June 11, 2021, four days after Paul and Maggie Murdaugh’s deaths, they were laid to rest. The double murder victims were laid to rest in Hampton, South Carolina’s Hampton Cemetery on June 11, 2021.

Paul’s great-great-grandfather Randolph Murdaugh and great-grandfather Randolph “Buster” Murdaugh are buried nearby where Paul’s mother and son are laid to rest. Paul’s grandfather, Randolph Murdaugh III, joined them three days later when he passed away at age 85 from chronic health problems.

Hundreds of People Attended the Funeral of Paul and Maggie Murdaugh

Hundreds of people, including local and state politicians, friends, and law police, attended the public burial conducted by the Murdaugh family for their two murdered loved ones. The Murdaugh family was helped by Peeples-Rhoden Funeral Home of Hampton Chapel in arranging a graveside service for June 11, 2021, at noon.
