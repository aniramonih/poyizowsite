Who is Randy Orton?

Randy Orton, a prominent figure in professional wrestling and acting, was born on April 1, 1980, in Knoxville, Tennessee. He currently holds a contract with WWE under the Raw brand, marking a significant chapter in his enduring career.

Acknowledged as one of the premier professional wrestlers in history, Orton has an illustrious track record, securing the esteemed position of a 14-time World Champion and Grand Slam Champion. Notably, his alliance with Riddle forms the formidable RK-Bro tag team.
