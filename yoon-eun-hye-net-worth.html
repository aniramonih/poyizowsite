FAQs about Yoon Eun Hye

Who is Yoon Eun Hye?

She is a South Korean actress, singer, and model, known for her roles in popular TV dramas like Coffee Prince and Princess Hours. She first gained fame as a member of the girl group Baby V.O.X.

How old is she?

Born on October 3, 1984, she is 39 years old as of 2024.

What are some notable achievements in Hye’s career?

She has received numerous awards, including the Best Actress award at the 19th Grimae Awards and multiple popularity awards for her roles in Coffee Prince and The Vineyard Man. Her performance in Missing You earned her critical acclaim as well.

Has she ever been involved in any controversies?

Yes, she faced allegations of plagiarism related to a design she presented on a Chinese fashion show, which she denied. She also had a contract dispute with her former management company, Eight Peaks, but it was eventually resolved amicably.
