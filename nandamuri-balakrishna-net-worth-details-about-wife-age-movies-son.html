Early Life and Education

News: Nandamuri Balakrishna, popularly known as Balayya, was born on June 10, 1960, in Madras, which is presently Chennai, Tamil Nadu. He hails from a family with strong ties to both the entertainment and political realms. His father, N. T. Rama Rao, was a renowned Telugu actor and a three-time Chief Minister of Andhra Pradesh, and his mother was Basavatarakam.

Balakrishna furthered his education at Nizam College in Hyderabad, where he earned a bachelor’s degree in commerce. Armed with a solid academic background, he embarked on a journey into the entertainment industry to uphold his family’s storied legacy.

Personal Life

In 1982, at the tender age of 22, Balakrishna entered into matrimony with Vasundhara Devi. Together, they have been blessed with three children – two daughters, Brahmani and Tejaswini, and a son named Taraka Rama Mokshagna Teja. Balakrishna’s family has been a steadfast source of encouragement and support throughout his life, be it in his personal or professional endeavors.

Career and Rise to Stardom

Balakrishna’s journey into the world of acting began in 1974 with the movie “Tatamma Kala,” which was directed by his father, N. T. Rama Rao. He started as a child actor and over time, progressed into more substantial roles. His breakthrough came in 1987 when he received widespread recognition for his role as Raghavendra Rao in the film “Apoorva Sahodarulu,” directed by K. Raghavendra Rao.

In 1991, Balakrishna achieved a significant breakthrough with the science-fiction film “Aditya 369,” directed by Singeetam Srinivasa Rao. This movie highlighted Balakrishna’s versatility and acting talent, garnering him substantial recognition and praise from both audiences and critics. Following this success, he continued to feature in a string of successful films, including “Alludu,” “Vijayendra Varma,” “Veerabhadra,” “Allari Pidugu,” “Okka Magaadu,” and “Maharathi.”

Balakrishna staged a remarkable comeback in 2010 with the film “Simha,” directed by Boyapati Srinu. This movie not only revitalized his career but also firmly established him as a top-tier actor in the industry. His portrayal in “Simha” received universal acclaim, leading to him receiving several accolades, including two Nandi Awards and two Filmfare Awards for Best Actor.

Political Career and Philanthropy

In addition to his thriving acting career, Balakrishna ventured into politics in 2014 when he assumed office as a member of the Andhra Pradesh Legislative Assembly, representing the Hindupur constituency. His entry into the political arena demonstrated his dedication to public service and his desire to instigate positive societal changes.

Balakrishna is deeply committed to philanthropy. He is the owner of the Basavatarakam Indo-American Cancer Hospital and Research Institute, which offers top-tier medical services to individuals in need. His remarkable efforts in the healthcare sector have earned him commendation, and he remains dedicated to enhancing society through his philanthropic initiatives.
