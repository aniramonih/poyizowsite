TinaKitten, a well-known Twitch streamer, content creator, gamer, and internet sensation hails from South Korea. She achieved fame through her live streaming activities on her ‘@TinaKitten’ Twitch channel.

TinaKitten’s Age, Biography & Profile Summary

On June 30, 1998, TinaKitten made her debut in this world. She is known by her full name Christina “Tina” Kenyon and falls under the zodiac sign of Cancer, making her 24 years old. Although born in South Korea, she later moved to Los Angeles, California, in the United States, making her half Korean and half white. While she has never discussed her parents publicly, it’s known that she has some family connection.

TinaKitten’s Weight and Height

Standing at 5 feet 5 inches and weighing 57 kilograms, TinaKitten boasts a slim physique and attractive figure. Her measurements are 31-24-32 inches for her chest, waist, and hips, with a shoe size of 6 (US). With dark brown eyes and brown hair, Tina exudes confidence and radiates optimism.

TinaKitten’s Career

TinaKitten gained widespread recognition through her gaming and artistic talents. Notably, her viral Among Us game, where she struggled to solve a simple Simon Says puzzle four times in a row, propelled her into the limelight. Her trademark quote during gaming streams, “Please!”, remains famous.

She made impromptu appearances on a podcast with Brofain and PeterparkTV and clinched victory in the 3rd Impostor World Championship of Among Us, besting Fuslie and Dakotaz in the finals. Tina is renowned for her drawing and gaming streams, accompanied by her distinct art style.

Often engaging in game sessions with friends, she plays a variety of games including Karl, Among Us, Stardew Valley, Minecraft, Raft, Foolish, Corpse, and Sykkuno, among others.

On September 15, 2021, Tina began her tenure as a content creator for 100T. Subtle hints on social media preceded the official announcement of her signing with 100T. Notably, on the day of the announcement, she conducted a cooking stream with prominent guests like BrookeAB, Fuslie, Kyedae, and Valkyrae.

She also became a member of the Dream SMP on October 23, 2021, and joined MCC on May 28, 2022, achieving the 9th position with her team (Foolish, Sapnap, and Michaelmcchill) while securing the 39th rank individually.

TinaKitten’s Boyfriend and Relationships

Tina was previously in a long-term relationship with Jummychu, also known as Jose Borromeo, before announcing their separation on March 2, 2021. She attributes much of her exposure and success in the OTV universe to Jummy and they maintain an amicable relationship. Currently, Tina is single and unattached.

During one of her Twitch streams, she revealed her identification as demisexual, recounting instances of confusion and uncertainty about her sexual orientation during high school.
