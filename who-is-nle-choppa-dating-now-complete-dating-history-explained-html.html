Who Is NLE Choppa Dating Now Complete Dating History Explained

Posted by Billy Koelling on Sunday, August 11, 2024

NLE Choppa’s Rise to Fame

NLE Choppa, known for his lively music and rap skills, has made quite a name for himself in the music industry. He gained recognition when his debut album, “Top Shotta,” stormed the music charts in 2020. With hit singles like “Camelot” and “Walk Em Down,” he quickly became a household name, securing a spot in the top ten of the US Billboard 200.

Mysteries Surrounding His Love Life

Besides his music, NLE Choppa’s love life has also grabbed the spotlight. Fans are curious to know if he’s currently dating someone. However, the rapper has remained tight-lipped about his personal affairs, and there are no credible sources or official statements confirming any romantic involvement in 2023.

A Glimpse into NLE Choppa’s Dating History

NLE Choppa’s dating history has been marked by highs and lows. He was previously in a relationship with Mariah, a love story that seemed inseparable. Mariah even appeared in some of his music videos, and he dedicated the song “Forever” to her. However, their love journey took an unexpected turn, resulting in a breakup. Rumors suggested that infidelity played a role in their separation, with Mariah alleging a near miss from a Draco.

Despite their split, NLE Choppa and Mariah welcomed their daughter, Clover, and decided to co-parent her, prioritizing their child’s well-being.

Later, the rapper caught the public’s eye with his relationship with Marissa Da’Nae, who is a prominent social media influencer known for her fashion and lifestyle content. Despite the significant age gap, they dated for a year, sharing affectionate posts on social media. Unfortunately, their love story ended on a bitter note.

In September 2022, NLE Choppa made headlines by announcing his single status on Twitter. Marissa, in response, shared her emotional struggles on Instagram, expressing the difficulties she faced post-breakup.

NLE Choppa’s Candid Confession

To address the breakup and the events leading up to it, NLE Choppa took to YouTube with a 37-minute video titled “Clearing the Air.” In the video, he took full responsibility for their split, admitting to his actions.

He revealed that he desired a polygamous relationship, while Marissa was not comfortable with the idea. This difference in perspective led to their separation. NLE Choppa acknowledged Marissa’s willingness to make sacrifices for their relationship but understood that it wasn’t aligned with her true desires.

The Meagan Good Episode

Following his breakup with Marissa, NLE Choppa publicly pursued Meagan Good, a 41-year-old actress. Despite the significant age gap, he showed a strong interest in her.
