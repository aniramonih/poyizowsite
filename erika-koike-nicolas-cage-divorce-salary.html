Erika Koike is an American professional makeup artist, best known for working in the makeup department of a short drama-horror film titled Hankikanto. Her relationship with the world-renowned actor Nicolas Cage that increased her popularity. She was the wife of Nicolas Cage for four days before he requested for annulment.

Who is Erika Koike?

38 years old, Erika Koike was born in 1983 in the United States of America.

Her parents own a chain of restaurants called ‘Jasmine Thai Cuisine’ in Southern California. Erika worked as a waitress in her parents' restaurant before shifting her attention towards becoming a professional makeup artist.

She has faced several criminal charges in her life. In 2006 she was charged with domestic violence against her then-husband. The domestic violence charge was dismissed, and she got a divorce in 2014.

In 2015, she fought with her neighbor. Her neighbor sued her for hitting on the face, but the case went nowhere.

In 2008 and 2011 in Los Angles and 2016 in Vegas, she had three DUI charges on different occasions. 

Erika Koike Net Worth & Salary

The national average salary for a makeup artist in the United States is $ 31.44 per hour, with a salary range between $7.25 and $94.45 per hour. But there is no exact idea about her net worth.

She has started her makeup career with Katja Niemi-directed Finnish short film Hankikanto. Erika's relationship with the world-renowned actor Nicolas Cage increased her popularity.

Erika Koike Relationship With Nicolas Cage 

Erika Koike started dating Nicolas Cage in the year 2018. Nicolas Cage is one of the names on the list of talented actors in Hollywood. He is also known as National Treasure Star. He was previously married to former waitress Alice Kim for 12 years, singer-songwriter Lisa Marie Presley for two years, and actress Patricia Arquette for six years.

Cage and Koike have mostly kept their relationship low-profile. Koike and Cage were first spotted together when they attended one of Carrot Top's shows in June 2016. Cage had just got separated from his third wife at the time. 

Erika and Nicolas were first linked when they were spotted vacationing together in Puerto Rico in April 2018. At that time, Cage was filming one of his films, Primal in Puerto Rico. Later, the couple was snapped on their way to having dinner in Los Angeles in May 2018.

In March of 2019, Nicolas and Erika were in Las Vegas. They get drunk and decided to get married. 

After dating for a year, Erika and Cage took their relationship to the next level by getting married on 27 March 2019 in Las Vegas. Their marriage didn’t last long. Their married life turned into one of the most short-lived unions in the history of weddings. Nicolas asked for a divorce just four days after the marriage.

Why Nicole Cage Wanted Divorce With Erika Koike?

According to People, Cage has requested the annulment because he was intoxicated at the time of the wedding ceremony and could not understand the long-term effects of his actions. 

Cage also annulled that Koike did not disclose her relationship with another person before the pair wed. Cage said that he found out Koike wasn’t precisely faithful, and she has a criminal history. He also claimed that he did not know about her criminal past and present before the marriage. 

After three months of annulment, the couple succeeded in getting a divorce in June 2019. 

The court report shows that Erika and Nicolas received their marriage certificate on the same day Cage went for annulment.

According to legal documents obtained by TMZ, Erika asked for spousal support, saying she lost her job opportunities being in a relationship with Nicolas Cage. She also asked for legal fees.
