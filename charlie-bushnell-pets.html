Birthday

June 16, 2004 (19)

Charlie Bushnell is an American actor known for his roles in Disney productions and beyond. He gained prominence for his portrayal of Bobby Cañero-Reed in the Disney series “Diary of a Future President” and is set to play Luke Castellan in the Disney+ adaptation of “Percy Jackson and the Olympians.” In addition to his work with Disney, Bushnell has expanded his acting repertoire by playing Hicks in the 2020 short film “Wetworks” and providing the voice for Chase in Mike Judge’s animated comedy series “In the Know.”
