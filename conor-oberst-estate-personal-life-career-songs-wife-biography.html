Conor Oberst is an American singer-songwriter, who has been a powerhouse in the world of indie music for years. This talented individual has been singing and writing songs since the tender age of 12 and currently, there is no turning back for this man. At 13, aspiring to become an industry star, he recorded his voice on cassette and came up with a self-released album titled ‘Water’. Soon Oberst, together with his brother, became the co-founder of the ‘Saddle Creek’ music label.

When you look closely at the history of rock bands, you can see that many successful artists don’t stay with the same group of musicians for their entire career. And that is precisely what Conor did. To date, this versatile musician has founded more than 8 bands but it is his work with the group ‘Bright Eyes’ that has earned him all the fame. Bright Eyes is one of the most critically acclaimed bands in the indie music genre. It is without a doubt Oberst’s best partnership to date. Let us now know more about this musical genius.

Conor Oberst: early life, education and career

Conor Oberst was born on February 15, 1980, to parents Mathew Ryan Oberst Sr. and Nancy Oberst. He grew up and spent most of his early years in Ohama, Nebraska. He grew up in a fairly affluent family with his father an information manager at Mutual of Omaha and his mother a director of elementary education for Omaha Public Schools.

For his education, Conor first went to St. Leo School in Omaha, but later moved on to Creighton Preparatory School. During his time at Creighton, Oberst began learning to play the guitar when he was only 10 years old. He was also part of different musical groups and choirs. Then, to complete his higher education, Conor joined the University of Nebraska. However, Conor later ended up dropping out to pursue a career in music.

In 1992, when Conor was only 12 years old, he was offered the opportunity to perform alongside American rock musicians Ted Stevens and Bill Hoover. At that stage, he had already started writing songs. This earned him a well-deserved reputation for being a talented young artist. A year later, he released his debut album ‘Water’, which was financed by his brother; the brothers even started their own label company.

Oberst had been writing singles for many years before he finally decided to form a band in 1994. He called the group, ‘The Faint’, and it had 6 members; the group managed to compose 9 songs before breaking up. Later, in 1995, he returned to co-found a new band, ‘Commander Venus’. They were able to release two albums, both released under Conor’s label.

Conor Oberst: bright eyes and achievements

In 1995, Oberst co-founded a band that would change his life. They called the band ‘Bright Eyes’ and it helped Oberst gain fame and success. Unlike most of his bands, Bright Eyes are still together and at the top of his career.

The main reason for Oberst’s rise in music is the band Bright Eyes. He has been with the group since 1995. In the early years, Bright Eyes used to be considered a unique project by many. Oh, they have proven everyone wrong! They went on to write albums like ‘Every Day and Every Night’, ‘Fevers and Mirrors’ and ‘Letting off the Happiness’. Some of his major works for Bright Eyes, including his songs and albums, are listed below.

His work on the band’s self-titled solo album ‘Monsters of Folk’ was released in 2009. This was ranked 15th on the year’s ‘Billboard Top 200’ music chart, which is probably one of his greatest achievements. Conor also received the “Peacemaker of the Year” award from the “Nebraskans for Peace” association. He was awarded this award in 2010 for his contribution to American music.

Conor Oberst: personal life

Conor is currently married to his loving wife Figueroa Escamilla. They said their wedding vows in 2010. However, according to some rumors, Oberst was romantically involved with Winona Ryder and Maria Taylor. Not much is known about these accusations, which are clearly not without merit, but as the phrase goes, “innocent until proven guilty.”

Conor grew up with two of his brothers, Justin and Mathew. His older brother Justin works as a partner in a law firm, while Mathew is a teacher and hobby musician.

Conor Oberst: Net Worth

This 38-year-old singer-songwriter has done incredibly well in his career and his net worth is estimated to be around $185 million. He amassed much of this fortune from music, but has had lucrative endorsement deals and is savvy with investments. Oberst also owns several restaurant chains in Washington; He even has shares in the ‘Omaha Angels’ football club.

Conor is also active on social media platforms where he has nearly 22,000 followers on Instagram.
