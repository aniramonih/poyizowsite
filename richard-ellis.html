Posted by Tandra Barner on Saturday, August 10, 2024

Richard Ellis Bio | Wiki

Richard Ellis is an American founding pastor of Reunion Church in Dallas TX which he co-founded in 1997. Reunion Church is a multi-ethnic, non-denominational church. Dallas needed a church like it and a pastor like Richard. The radio show and the websitewww.RichardEllisTalks.com joined the Reunion Church community under the leadership of Richard.

He served for three years as Minister to Single Adults at the First Baptist Church of Dallas, Texas. For ten years Richard served as the featured speaker for Lunch Talk, a weekly business luncheon, at the First Baptist Church of Dallas. He can be heard in Dallas on 100.7 FM weekdays at 6:30 am and 9:30 pm and Sundays at 10:30 am. He is one of the featured stories on iamsecond.com.

Richard Ellis Age

Richard Ellis Height

Richard Ellis Family

He was born in the United States to missionary parents. Richard lived in England as a child while his father was studying at Oxford, then moved to Brazil where his parents were missionaries for many years. Later, he came back to the states to ﬁnish his education. He is the sixth generation of pastors in his family.

Richard Ellis Wife

Richard is married to his beautiful wife Rebecca. Together, the couple shares three daughters Elizabeth, Victoria, and Catherine, and lives in Dallas. Richard loves an occasional round of golf. And he loves the Dallas Cowboys.

Richard Ellis Education

He graduated from Baylor University in 1982, with a BA in Oral Communications and Southwestern Baptist Theological Seminary in 1985 with an MDiv. Richard also did some of his Master’s work at Dallas Theological Seminary. After graduating from seminary, he went into business for 10 years trying to spend some time working on his personal relationship with Christ and not relying on his religious upbringing.

Richard Ellis Reunion Church | Church

In 1997 Richard Ellis, along with 14 others, planted Reunion Church, a multi-cultural, multi-ethnic, non-denominational church in a small office, in the heart of Dallas, Texas.  A group of 15 believers, devoted to the Lord, shared a common mission to reach the ALL of Dallas with the truth of the Gospel of Jesus Christ.

Embracing the multi-cultural and multi-ethnic diversity of the city, the church sought to be a place where anyone could come to hear about Jesus, plant roots in His truth, and learn how to live lives that bring glory to God.
