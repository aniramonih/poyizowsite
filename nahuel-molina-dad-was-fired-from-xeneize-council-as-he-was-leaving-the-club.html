One of his dads deposed the Boca leadership?

Hugo Molina, Nahuel’s father, was sacked upon his departure from the club at the Xeneize Council. According to reports, he and his agent, Leo Rodriguez, met with a new committee. Roman Riquelme, Raul Cascini, the Patron, Jorge Bermudez, and Chelo Delgado arrived at the moment. They allegedly offered a contract and then made two bids.

However, they were unable to resolve their disputes. Nahuel was also summoned, and he expressed a desire to play at Boca. Hugo blew out at the Soccer Council member right then and there.

Hugo also mentioned this, noting

“Have you seen how Bermudez and Cascini performed? Is this how they behave while negotiating?”

His father even mentioned not just his son, but other sons who had been in the same predicament as Nahuel.
