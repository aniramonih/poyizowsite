The Journey Begins

Josh Widdicombe, that guy with the curly hair and a knack for turning everyday grievances into comedy gold, didn’t just stumble into the spotlight.

After honing his intellect at the University of Manchester, where he studied sociology and linguistics, Josh took a detour into sports journalism before the comedy bug bit hard.

From Sports Journalism to Stand-up Stardom

Before Josh became the king of comedy we know and adore, he was navigating the world of sports journalism. Yes, you read that right!
