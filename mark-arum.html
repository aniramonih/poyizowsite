Mark Arum Biography

Mark Arum is an American traffic and sports reporter at WSB-TV on Channel 2 Action News who was born and brought up in the United States of America. He has been a member of Triple Team Traffic since September 1997. In addition to anchoring B98.5FM traffic coverage, he also lends his voice to traffic reports on WSB. He began as the host of “The Mark Arum Show” in the year 2001, and it became part of WSB’s weekly line-up in the year 2014.

Before, he served as a sports writer in New York and Connecticut for eight years, covering professional and collegiate sports prior to moving to Atlanta. Mark graduated from Marist College in New York with a degree in journalism and public relations in the year 1996 and has appeared on CNN, MSNBC, ABC Nightly News, and Inside Edition.
