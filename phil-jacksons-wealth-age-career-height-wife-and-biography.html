Phil Jackson's Wealth, Age, Career, Height, Wife, and Biography

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 12, 2024

Here’s the latest on Phil Jackson’s net worth, age, career, height, wife, and biography.

Phil Jackson, a renowned American former professional basketball player, coach, and executive, has made a substantial impact on the world of basketball. His net worth, estimated to be $80 million in 2023, reflects not only his financial success but also his significant contributions to the sport. Let’s explore the life and career of this basketball icon.

Phil Jackson’s Profile Summary

Phil Jackson’s Early Life and Education

Phil Jackson, born on September 17, 1945, in Deer Lodge, Montana, was raised in a family of Assemblies of God ministers, Charles and Elisabeth Funk. His early passion for basketball paved the way for his remarkable achievements in the sport.

He attended high school in Williston, North Dakota, and later played college basketball for the University of North Dakota, where he was a member of the Sigma Alpha Epsilon fraternity. His college years laid the groundwork for his future success in professional basketball.

Phil Jackson’s Early Career

Phil Jackson’s professional basketball journey commenced in 1967 when he was drafted in the second round by the New York Knicks. This marked the beginning of his illustrious career as a player in the NBA.

He played for the New York Knicks until 1978, following which he signed with the New Jersey Nets and played for them until 1980. His exceptional skills on the court earned him a reputation as a formidable player.

Phil Jackson’s Coaching Career

After retiring as a player in 1978, Phil Jackson started his coaching career with the New Jersey Nets, where he served as their coach until 1981, showcasing his ability to excel in different aspects of the sport.

Throughout his coaching career, Phil Jackson had affiliations with several prominent teams, including the Piratas de Quebradillas, Gallegos de Isabela, and Chicago Bulls. His coaching expertise contributed to these teams’ remarkable victories.

Phil Jackson’s Accolades and Awards

Phil Jackson’s incredible talent and hard work have earned him numerous awards and titles throughout his career. Noteworthy accolades include the NBA All-Rookie First Team, NBA Champion, First-team Division II All-American, and First-team All-NCC.

As a coach, Phil Jackson led his teams to victory in the NBA Championships an astounding 11 times, making him a vital asset to the teams he mentored.

Phil Jackson’s Net Worth

Phil Jackson’s net worth of $80 million in 2023 reflects his significant achievements on and off the court, primarily stemming from his career as a player, coach, and executive.

Phil Jackson’s Lucrative Contracts

His $60 million, five-year contract as the president of the Knicks played a pivotal role in boosting his net worth.

Phil Jackson’s Height

Standing at 6’8″ and weighing 220lbs, Phil Jackson commands attention both on and off the court. His Zodiac sign is Virgo, and he identifies as a straight male.

Phil Jackson’s Wife and Relationships

Phil Jackson was previously married to Maxine Jackson (1967-1972) and June Jackson (1974-2000). He is a father to five children—Chelsea, Charlie, Brooke, Ben, and Elizabeth Jackson.

Phil Jackson’s Current Role

Phil Jackson, a Hall of Famer, currently serves as the President of Basketball Operations for the New York Knicks.

Phil Jackson’s Championship Rings

Phil Jackson holds a record 13 championship rings, making him the coach with the most NBA rings ever.

Phil Jackson’s Coaching Earnings

Phil Jackson earned $9 million coaching the Bulls for six championship wins and later signed a $30 million, five-year contract with the Lakers.

Phil Jackson’s Titles as a Player

Phil Jackson won an NBA title with the Knicks in ’73 and a CBA championship with Albany in ’84, earning Coach of the Year honors.

Reason for Phil Jackson’s Departure from the Bulls

Phil Jackson left the Bulls after 1997-98 due to a strained relationship with GM Jerry Krause.
