Amy Freeze is an accomplished American meteorologist with six Emmy awards to her name. She currently serves as an anchor on the ad-supported streaming weather service for FOX Weather. Prior to joining FOX Weather, she worked at WABC.

Key Facts about Amy Freeze

Amy’s Personal Information

Amy was born on June 19, 1974, in Utah, America to Linda and Bill Freeze. She attended Jeffersonville High School and graduated with a degree in Communication from Brigham Young University. Amy is American and of Caucasian descent, standing at five feet, six inches, with a slim body and weighing 66kg.

Amy’s Career

Amy Freeze is an anchor for FOX Weather and has previously worked at WABC and WFLD-TV in Chicago, Illinois. She has also contributed to weather programs in various other cities and has received numerous awards for her exceptional work in meteorology.

Details about Amy’s Illness

Amy had to take time off from her television duties due to an undisclosed illness. She mentioned that she was recovering from surgery, sparking speculation among her fans that she may have contracted COVID-19.

Amy Freeze’s Personal Life and Divorce

Amy was previously married to Gary Arbuckle, with whom she has four children. However, the couple divorced in 2016 after 20 years of marriage. The specific reason for their divorce remains private, but they continue to co-parent their children together.
