Broken Vessels Lyrics

Posted by Kelle Repass on Sunday, August 11, 2024

Broken Vessels by Hillsong Worship is a heartfelt anthem that beautifully captures the essence of surrender and redemption.

With its powerful lyrics and soul-stirring melodies, the song invites listeners on a journey of healing and restoration.
