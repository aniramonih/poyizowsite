Brad Pitt Unveils How He Visited Alcoholics Anonymous

Actor Brad Pitt told GQ magazine how he attended Alcoholics Anonymous in 2016.

“I saw other people who were addicted when they poured out their soul, and it’s just terrible for me,” he shared.
