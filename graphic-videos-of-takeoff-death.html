Graphic Videos Of Takeoffs Death Revealed The Truth Behind His Death

Posted by Kelle Repass on Saturday, August 17, 2024

Takeoff’s fans were not happy after they saw the graphic video of Takeoff’s death circulating on the social media platform.

Takeoff died at 28; he was a famous rapper and member of the Migos.

He received many awards and popularity from his band, along with his uncle Quavo and cousin Offset.

On the early morning of Tuesday, 1st November 2022, an Unknown man opened gunfire on him outside the Houston Bowling Alley. Many people came across the bowling alley street after hearing gunshots.

In the following hour, several people captured the moment of the incident on their mobile, and they shared and posted Graphic videos of Takeoff’s death on their social media accounts. 

In the footage, the famous boxer Shakur Stevenson was also spotted standing near him and Takeoff’s uncle and fighter getting into a heated argument on the social media comment session. 

They faced criticism from fans and top artists after these videos went viral. 

In the video, “All moments were captured in the video, including audio of Quavo, who called 911 at his death time. People saw that his pain was uncontrollable and he shouted for help. On the video, at least ten multiple gunshots were heard.’

The camera appeared to be moving away from the crowds just seconds before multiple gunshots were heard. 

People were fleeing from the area after hearing the sound of firing.

His uncle Quavo was also present at the time of his death, and his traumatized crying voice was also captured when he called his name, which went viral on social media. 

On Tuesday afternoon Police Chief Troy Finner said in his press conference, ‘We are asking the people who were during the incident happened to provide us with more information and videos. I know it was quite disturbing but we want more videos for our investigation.’

Graphic Videos Of Takeoff’s Death were provided to police by TMZ and other people who were present at that time. The videos and photos received criticism from the audience.
