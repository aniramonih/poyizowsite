Inside Fox News Anchor Dagen Mcdowell's Married Life, Family and Net Worth

Posted by Patria Henriques on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Mary Dagen McDowell, famously Dagen McDowell, is renowned as Fox Business Network's anchor and analyst. She parallelly works as a business correspondent for the Fox News Channel.

McDowell has hosted several shows since being associated with Fox. Some of them include Mornings with Maria, Imus in the Morning, and Outnumbered.

Prior to landing the reporting gig at Fox News, she worked for Smartmoney.com and the magazine of the same name.

Besides having a successful professional life, the 52-year-old correspondent also relishes an equally happy personal life. She is the wife of Fox News economic analyst Jonas Max Ferris.

Her Personal Life with Husband

McDowell met her now-husband Ferris in 2001 when they worked together in the show Cashin' In.

In Fox's show on May 6, 2011, the couple even talked about their comradery in their initial days of friendship. At first, Ferris revealed that he and McDowell met at Fox station 10 years ago.

"We were sort of handpicked. At least I think I was handpicked, I don't know about Dagen," Ferris added. "To basically argue in a mutual fun faceoff on the show...cast to because of our argumentative nature."

Interestingly, she was married to another man at that time, and Ferris got familiar with that detail very soon. However, Ferris was never married before he married the Fox anchor.

Since McDowell hadn't had a great relationship with her first husband, she eventually got a divorce sometime later.

After her divorce, she reportedly tied the knot with Ferris in 2005. Since then, they have been living together happily.

The couple, however, is very private about their personal life, keeping all their relationship timeline under wraps. McDowell and her husband also have not confirmed having their children yet.

Who Is McDowell's Husband Ferris?

McDowell's husband, Ferris, is an economist, financial analyst, and investment advisor by profession. He is also the founder of Maxfunds.com.

He has extensively written financial advice and investment columns for media outlets such as New York Post, Fox News, and Fox Business News.

Ferris also has made appearances on several business shows, including Bulls and Bears, Your World with Neil Cavuto, and Cavuto on Business.

McDowell Graduated from the Wake Forest University

The Bulls and Bears host was born on January 7, 1969, in Brookneal, Virginia, United States, to American parents with an Irish background.

On June 1, 2017, McDowell revealed through Twitter that her parents had been married for 56 years, and she was proud to be their daughter.

The Fox anchor graduated from Wake Forest University with a degree in Art History. She began her career as a financial journalist at the Institutional Investor's Newsletter Division.

Her Net Worth and Salary

McDowell joined Fox News as an anchor in September 2007. She previously was a business contributor for the Fox News Channel, starting in 2003.

She made a headline in August 2018 after her remarks regarding former president Donald Trump and the Muller Investigation on her show Outnumbered went viral.

How in the world could he ever cooperate and sit down with Mueller for an interview knowing that if you tell one lie to Bob Mueller, he will move to file charges?

With a successful career, McDowell has earned a net worth of $5 million with reportedly an annual salary of $350 thousand.

Her present husband, Ferris, on the other hand, also has a net worth of $5 million, Celebrity Net Worth claims.
