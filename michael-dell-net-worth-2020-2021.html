Career

He named his company Dell Computer Corporation and moved to North Austin to a business center.

Dell turned out to be the youngest chief executive of a company placed on the list of the top 500 companies of the Fortune magazine in 1992. In a very short time, Dell Inc. reported nearly $1 million in sales on an every-day basis from dell.com. In 1998, he created MSD Capital L.P. to deal with the investments of his family.
