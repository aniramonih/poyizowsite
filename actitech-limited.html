All there is to know about Actitech Limited

Posted by Tandra Barner on Monday, August 26, 2024

Over the space of the last two decades, Actitech Limited has grown to become a major Information Communication Technology (ICT) solutions provider in Nigeria, with major projects executed in different parts of the country.

This piece, therefore, focuses on the indigenous company and what type of services it offers across Nigeria.

What is Actitech Limited?

Actitech Limited was founded in 1993, with its head office located in Abuja. The company partners with leading manufacturers and technology firms to meet the unique ICT requirements of its clients within Nigeria and beyond.

Actitech is a fully indigenous, innovative ICT service provider and systems integrator with over 50 employees, many of whom are highly skilled and qualified engineers. It offers cutting-edge solutions to many organisations and people and aids them in achieving their goals in business, government, and lifestyle every day. The company prides itself in promoting and ensuring operational excellence while having a great influence on its neighbourhood.

According to its profile, Acitech is committed to long-term tech development and providing value to its clients, while ensuring that success will be fueled by its commitment to the highest standards of business conduct, surpassing commitments, offering new technology solutions and being its customers’ first choice.

What does Actitech limited sell?

Actitech Limited delivers computer network solutions for mission-critical transactions, applications, and services for maximizing business productivity.

Actitech Limited also specialises in the design, sales, supply, installation, integration and maintenance of a wide range of Information and Communications Technology Systems.

Actitech Limited reviews

Just like any other company, Actitech Limited has both good and bad reviews. Listed below are some of the reviews about the company.

A reviewer stated that Actitech is a good learning environment for anyone with a serious attitude because the business is willing to take on any task involving system integrations. Their ability to provide on short notice has been their main asset over the past few years, and it has helped them succeed.

Another reviewer stated that one of the problems the company has is that they lack a real established work culture, a normal work welfare culture, and a reward policy. It was occasionally challenging to manage projects and employees because the organisation is not project-oriented, making it tough to make some decisions or carry out initiatives in a planned manner, the reviewer wrote.

Someone else mentioned that another flaw of the company was that the reward system was poorly defined and the policy implementation method in the company is average. The reviewer added that the workplace can occasionally be stressful.
