Early Life

Will Arnett was born and brought up in Toronto, Ontario on 4th May 1970 and thus making him a Canadian citizen by birth. Will’s father was Emerson James, and their mother was Edith Alexandra. Arnett was not the only child to his parents, but he had one younger brother and two older sisters.

Arnett was born in a well-to-do family and was brought up in a lavish and luxury environment. Will’s father Emerson James was a corporate lawyer along with the CEO of the ‘Molson Breweries’. Arnett, in his early life, did his schooling from the ‘Lakefield College School.’ Apart from studies, Arnett had a keen interest in theatre classes. Thus, he started taking the theatre classes from the ‘Tarragon Theatre.’

Besides these activities, Will has attended other high school and colleges, i.e. ‘Leaside High School’ and the ‘Concordia University.’ Later in the year 1990, Will was relocated to the New York City and pursued his acting course from the ‘Lee Strasberg Theatre and Film Institute.’
