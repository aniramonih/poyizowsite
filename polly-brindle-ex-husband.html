Who Is Polly Brindle's Ex-Husband? Is She Dating Anyone Now?

Who is Polly Brindle's ex-husband?

One Selling the OC cast member who is getting a lot of attention online is Polly Brindle, an English former model who worked for major brands like Dior and Aston Martin. Polly moved to Los Angeles in 2011, and she has been immersed in luxury markets ever since.

Now, as she's taking center stage as part of the Selling the OC cast, many want to know more about Polly's ex-husband.

On the show, Polly discussed her marriage, and her divorce.

"All of my twenties, I was married," she said during a group dinner on the series premiere ("Paradise Comes with a Price"). "Like, I was playing the wifey, doing that thing."

When Austin asked if the Polly and her ex split up because they wanted to go their "separate ways," she said that it was more dramatic than that.

"No, it was a plane crash ending," she said.

In her confessional, Polly elaborated on what she meant by the "plane crash ending" statement.

"We took vows, and I took those vows very seriously. I got married for the rest of my life, and he was unfaithful," she said in her interview. "I saw pictures on his phone of him with two women. They were prostitutes."

Polly's experience with infidelity is part of the reason why she was so upset with Kayla's actions toward married co-star Tyler Stanaland on the show.

Though Polly didn't mention her husband by name on the Netflix program, her Instagram account does provide some clues about his potential identity.

In January of 2017, Polly shared a post on the app to celebrate the birthday of the "hottest husband," Stephen Wolf. Three years later, in January of 2020 she celebrated his 40th birthday with another post, referring to him as "birthday bae."

Will Polly be dating anyone on 'Selling the OC'?

At the moment, it doesn't seem like Polly is in an active relationship. Instead, she seems to be more focused on her career, and on building out a robust social life filled with friends.

Polly's Instagram page is largely dedicated to the time she spends with friends and other large groups of people.
