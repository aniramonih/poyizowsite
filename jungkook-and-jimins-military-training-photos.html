Jungkook and Jimin's Military Training Captured in Stunning Shots

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 6, 2024

Pictures of Jungkook and Jimin from their time in basic military training surfaced. Photos of BTS members Jimin and Jungkook in basic military training have recently gone viral, captivating fans all over the globe. Wearing helmets with the numbers 59 and 60, respectively, Jungkook and Jimin can be seen side by side in the group photos. Images also surfaced of the two international superstars practicing their shooting techniques in full military regalia.
