Freddie Fox Bio (Wiki)

Posted by Kelle Repass on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Who is Freddie Fox?

Freddie Fox is a British actor. He came into the limelight after appearing as singer Marilyn in the 2009 television film Why Didn’t They Ask Evans? and Worried About the Boy in 2010.

Furthermore, he has also played in various hit movies such as The Three Musketeers, King Arthur: Legend of the Sword, Cucumber, and many more.

Additionally, he has also won the third prize at the Ian Charleson Awards for his role in Romeo and Juliet in 2016.

Freddie Fox: Early Life, Childhood, and Education

Freddie was born on 5 April 1989, in Hammersmith, London, United Kingdom. He is the son of Edward Fox and Joanna David.

Furthermore, he grew up along with his siblings Emilia Fox and Lucy Preston. Talking about his nationality, he is British and his ethnicity is mixed.

Concerning his education, he joined Arnold House School and later moved to St John’s Wood area of London and Bryanston School.

In 2010, the British actor completed his graduation from Guildhall School of Music and Drama in London.

Freddie Fox: Career, Net Worth, and Awards

Freddie began his career with the 2009 television film Worried About the Boy. In the same year, he also played in the movie called St Trinian’s 2: The Legend of Fritton’s Gold. Later that year, he appeared in two more TV shows, Any Human Heart and This September.

Furthermore, he got huge recognition after featuring in movies such as Cucumber and Banana in 2015. Recently, the British actor has played in a couple of movies such as Dennis & Gnasher: Unleashed!, Watership Down, and Joe Orton Laid Bare in 2017.

Additionally, Freddie has also done some popular films which consist of The Three Musketeers and Pride. Back in 2015, he played a brilliant role in the 2015 movie Victor Frankenstein.

Recently, he has also appeared in the 2017 movies King Arthur: Legend of the Sword and Fanny Lye Deliver’d in 2017. Moreover, he has also played in some famous theatre shows such as Romeo and Juliet, The Juda Kiss, and A Flea in Her Ear.

Being a renowned actor, he pockets a massive amount of money from his profession. However, his salary is unknown. As of 2022, he has an estimated net worth of $1.5 million.

So far, Freddie has won the third prize at the Ian Charleson Awards for his role in Romeo and Juliet in 2016.
