'Teen Mom 2': What is Briana DeJesus's net worth? Mom of 2 is a 'go-getter'

Posted by Kelle Repass on Friday, August 16, 2024

Being one of the newer cast members on MTV's 'Teen Mom 2', Briana DeJesus won't make as much as her fellow counterparts do, since some of them have been on the reality show since it began. 

Viewers were first introduced to a bubbly DeJesus on an episode of '16 and Pregnant', and for a brief time on 'Teen Mom 3'. MTV knocked on her door to join the cast of 'Teen Mom 2' in 2017. She also told E! News that she was beyond excited to be living her life on-screen, saying "It's basically the same, but now I'm a little older, a little more mature. It's still the same in my eyes, though. It's like [the crew] never left!". 

Talking money, DeJesus has done pretty decently for herself in her reality tv based career. The star is worth $750,000, according to CheatSheet. At the beginning of it all, DeJesus was making only about $20,000 per season, but her salary has since increased to $100,000 per season. In addition, she also works full-time in the corporate field, and part-time at a beauty salon!

The hustling mother of two moved from an apartment to a townhome, which is one of her most significant and lavish purchases to date. 

According to The Sun, the reality star put an offer on the townhouse in November 2019 for $269,000. The new space is over 2,000 square feet, with four bedrooms and bathrooms! Talk about living life king size. 

More often than not, people are still surprised to know that DeJesus has an actual 9-5 job, given that most 'Teen Mom' stars bank on the reality show's consistent paychecks to make a living. 

When 'Teen Mom 3' ended in November 2013, DeJesus decided to get a headstart on her professional life. While she had the option of just promoting products on Instagram like many do these days, she chose to take up a job in the timeshare industry. In November 2017,  she said in a since-deleted tweet, "I work in timeshare. I deal with the backhand work of it." (via InTouch Weekly). 

It sure looks like there's nothing stopping this determined woman.

Season 11 of 'Teen Mom 2' premieres on March 8, 8 pm ET, on MTV.
