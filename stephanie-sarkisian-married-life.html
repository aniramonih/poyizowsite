Stephanie Sarkisian Married Life

Posted by Patria Henriques on Wednesday, August 21, 2024

Hey, did you know? 

According to psychologists and philosophers, if a relationship lasts 7 years, it will last a lifetime. But, the quote bound to be just a line of lies for Stephanie Sarkisian and her ex-husband Steve Sarkisian. 

They were together not only for seven but for 19 years, yet they separated. So, the question is how was her married life with Steve Sarkisian, an American football coach? 

Stephanie Sarkisian: A Divorcee Of Steve Sarkisian

Stephanie Sarkisian lived a happily married life with her former husband Steve Sarkisian for around two decades from 1997 to 2016. The couple knew each other since their childhood. They started to date later in their high school days. They got married a weekend before her beau left for the Canadian Football League's training camp with Saskatchewan. 

Stephine and her now ex-husband Steve Sarkisian share three children; two daughters named Ashley Sarkisian &Taylor Sarkisian and son Brady Sarkisian. 

After around 18 years of their blissful married life, the pair announced their separation back in April 2015. The news, as a matter of fact, shocked lots of people, the Conquest Chronicles/SB Nation reported. Moreover, in the interview, Steve Sarkisian said: 

"It is our desire to protect the privacy of our children and each other. Our goal is to raise our children in a joint, cooperative style as caring and loving parents. Thank you for respecting our family's privacy. We will have no further comment."

Their divorce was finalized a year later in 2016. And if you are wondering about some financial settlements, I am here to clear your doubts. 

Well, it can be said that Steve Sarkisian was highly favored in the divorce settlement. Her ex-spouse had to pay a one-time check for $293 thousand and a Land Rover as a part of the settlement. 

A source reported that Stephanie Sarkisian agreed on no spousal support, no child support, and joint custody of their 3 minor kids. The same site revealed that there was a private settlement in regards to the bulk of the family fortune, however, none of the parties have discussed about the actual figures. 

Stephanie Sarkisian Net Worth

As of March 2021, Stephanie Sarkisian is estimated to have a net worth of $100 thousand. The only source of income known to date is the one-time check of $293 thousand given by her ex-husband as the divorce settlement. 

According to s reliable site, before her marriage to Steve Sarkisian, Stephanie used to work as a part-time teacher. 

On the other hand, her ex-husband Steve Sarkisian who is an American football coach and the former quarterback is estimated to have a fortune of around $2.8 million, as of March 2021.
