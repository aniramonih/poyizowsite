Posted by Tandra Barner on Sunday, August 18, 2024

Actor Luis Guzmán was born in Puerto Rico on August 28, 1956. His nearly 40-year career has included a lot of movies and television shows.

Guzmán was raised in Greenwich Village and the nearby Lower East Side in New York City after being born in Cayey, Puerto Rico. His stepfather, Benjamin Cardona, was a TV repairman, and his mother, Rosa, worked in a hospital.

Guzmán received his degree from The American University, started working as a social worker soon after, and finally got very interested in street theater and indie movies.
