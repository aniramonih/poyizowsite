Joel Kinnaman Bio, Wife, Height, Age, Net worth, Family

Posted by Martina Birk on Tuesday, August 20, 2024

Joel Kinnaman is an American-Swedish actor best known for his fantastic portrayals of Johan Westlund in JW in the Easy Money film series, a Swedish crime drama. He began his ascent in the entertainment industry in the 1990’s as a child actor starring in twenty-two (22) episodes of the soap opera Storstad before taking a hiatus from college.

After a lengthy absence he returned in 2002 and appeared in the thriller, The Invisible Man and from then on he has appeared in many theatres, soap operas and mainstream films and TV series some of which include Hannah med H , Crime and Punishment , Me Skuggan av varmen translated literally In your favorite series Easy Money, The Darkest Hour, The Killing, Safe House, House of Cards, Hanna among others .

Joel Kinnaman’s Bio and Age

He was born Charles Joel Nordstrom Kinnaman on November 25, 1979 in Stockholm, Sweden. Joel Kinnaman received his high school education in Del Valle, Texas. He was an exchange student for a year.

Though he began digging down his craft as an actor as a child, he eventually left the limelight and returned in 2002. His fame peaked in 2009 and since then he has appeared in several box office blockbusters some of which are RoboCop (2014), Suicide Squad (2016), The Informant (2019). He portrayed Erik Heller in the American web action series “Drama” Hanna.

His 2019 credits include The Sound of Philadephia, The Secret We Keep and For All Mankin D. The amazing Swedish native has not only earned recognition and tangible fame but has also been nominated for a number of awards such as the Saturn and a Swedish Guldbaggage award for his roles in The Killing and Easy Money.

How much does Joel Kinnaman make from his net worth?

Joel Kinnaman has appeared in many blockbuster films, many of which have garnered tremendous numbers at the box office worldwide. While Suicide Squad made over $747 million, RoboCopgrossed $ 53.4 million. Thanks to these accomplishments, Kinnaman was able to amass a net worth of $10 million. He is indeed one of Hollywood’s millionaires.

Family – parents, siblings and wife

His father’s name is Steve Kinnaman, a name he changed from David Kinnaman. Joel Kinnaman’s father was an American soldier posted to Bangkok during the Vietnam War. His mother’s name is Bitte Kinnaman and she is a therapist.

Thanks to his parents, Joel has a multiracial background; Jewish-Swedish descent from his mother, and German, Irish, Scottish, and American descent from his father. He is very fluent in Swedish and English.

He has five sisters, including Leyla Belle Drake and actress Melinda Kinnaman, who is perhaps the most famous of them all.

Joel Kinnaman and his wife Cleo Wattenstrom, a renowned Swedish tattoo artist, are separated. The split was announced sometime in 2018 with no clear reason behind the decision, however the two don’t have bad blood between them as they have remained good friends.

The couple reportedly met and started dating in early 2014, but made their first public appearance in August 2014. By December 2014, the media had touches of the relationship. The hype continued until April 2016 when the duo met at a venue called Same Day Marriage In San Vincent Boulevard, Los Angeles, California, USA Unfortunately, the lovebirds were separated by 2018, and the details of their divorce were unclear to the media. Cleo Wattenstrom messaged Joel via social media on his birthday in November 2018.

Prior to his marriage to Cleo, Joel reportedly dated Olivia Munn, the mother of two, an X-Men actress for about two years, and Jane Timglass, an actress he met on set.

Interestingly, Joel Kinnaman has recently been spotted in the company of the beautiful Swedish Victorian Secret model Kelly Gale. Both were spotted on Sunday January 20th, 2019. The couple has yet to make a statement as to whether or not it’s a relationship.

Height

Joel Kinnaman is obviously a handsome man with his beautiful greenish eyes coupled with a great athletic body. He stands at a staggering height of 1.89m supported by a matching body mass of 92kg.
