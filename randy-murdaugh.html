Randy Murdaugh Parents & Siblings

Randolph Murdaugh IV, widely known as Randy Murdaugh, was born in 1966 to his mother, Libby Murdaugh, and father, Randolph Murdaugh III, and raised in Hampton, South Carolina, where he grew up with his brothers Alex Murdaugh, John Marvin, and Lynn Murdaugh Goettee.

He attended public schools in the area before moving on to higher education. He received his undergraduate degree from the University of South Carolina and then attended law school at the same institution.

Alex and Randy were born two years apart, attended the same university for their undergraduate and law degrees, and worked together at the family law firm their great-grandfather established over a century ago.

But even in their college days, their differences were evident. Alex was a football player and a party-goer, while Randy preferred to spend his weekends’ hunting and fishing in their hometown of Hampton, South Carolina.
