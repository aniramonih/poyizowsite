Andrea Espada Wiki: Age, Early Life, and Education

Born Andrea Rincon Espada on the 17th August 1986, in Columbia, she is currently 32 years old; she hasn’t revealed much about her early life, including the names of her parents and their vocation, while she has also remained silent on whether she has any siblings or not. All that we know is that she went to the Autonomous University of the West, majoring in marketing and international business.

Career Beginnings

Following her graduation, Andrea launched her career in her native Columbia, but after only a few months she moved to the USA, feeling that she would have a better chance of succeeding in there rather than Columbia. She experienced a slow start, with only a couple of jobs offered to her, nevertheless, she had her mind set on success so started a Vine channel, and with short comedy videos, quickly became popular. It was in 2009 that she made her first appearance on screen, in the short film “Jack the Reaper”.

Rise to Prominence

Gradually Andrea was becoming more popular, and it was in 2013 that she had her first breakthrough, when she was named as the guest-host of the show “Estrella TV Presenta: Esto es Guerra”. The same year, Andrea featured in show “Noches con Platanito”, and returned to the show in 2018. To speak further of her accomplishments, Andrea became known throughout the world thanks to hosting the show “UFC Now”, which position she has held for quite some time, which has significantly increased her wealth.
